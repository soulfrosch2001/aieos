# Design Review Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: design-director
Extends: architecture-council

## Purpose
Decides whether a project's design is coherent and good enough to pass a phase gate — schematic, design development, or pre-permit. It judges design intent, quality, and cross-discipline coherence. It does **not** touch schedule and budget (the [project-director](../../01-executive/project-director/)'s domain), engineering sufficiency (proven in [03-technical](../../03-technical/)), or regulatory compliance (the [code-compliance-council](../code-compliance-council/)'s gate).

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [design-director](../../01-executive/design-director/) — holds the design veto.
- **Core** (must attend): [lead-architect](../../02-design/lead-architect/), [concept-architect](../../02-design/concept-architect/), [project-architect](../../04-delivery/project-architect/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [sustainability-designer](../../02-design/sustainability-designer/), [structural-engineer](../../03-technical/structural-engineer/), [bim-specialist](../../03-technical/bim-specialist/), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).

## When convened
At each design phase gate, sized by tier — see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). **T2** gets a council sign-off; **T3** strategic projects need council plus the [principal](../../01-executive/principal/). The [studio-orchestrator](../../01-executive/studio-orchestrator/) convenes; the council is not standing.

## Inputs
- The design package for the gate — drawings, model, and concept narrative.
- Open items and constraints from [03-technical](../../03-technical/) and the delivery team.
- Prior decisions in [design-decisions](../../memory/registers/design-decisions.md) and relevant [design-lessons](../../memory/registers/design-lessons.md).
