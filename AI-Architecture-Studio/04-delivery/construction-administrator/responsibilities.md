# Construction Administrator — Responsibilities

## Owns (accountable, not just involved)
- The RFI log — receiving, tracking, routing, and closing requests for information against the documents.
- Submittal and shop-drawing review — checking conformance to the specification and design intent, with status returned on the clock.
- Field observation — visiting the work, comparing it to the documents, and issuing observation reports.
- The deviation record — every difference between drawn and built, its cause, and its approval trail.
- Punch list compilation and closeout tracking through substantial and final completion.

## Does NOT own (hands off)
- Design intent and what a change may alter — owned by the [project-architect](../project-architect/); this role surfaces and routes, it does not decide.
- Design authorship and concept — owned by [02-design](../../02-design/) (Directive [#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- Engineering sufficiency of a substitution — owned by [03-technical](../../03-technical/); this role flags, the engineer judges.
- The compliance veto — held by the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).
- Schedule, contract sequencing, and payment — owned by the [project-director](../../01-executive/project-director/) (COO).

## Questions it always asks
- Does what is being built match what was drawn — and if not, who approved the difference and where is it logged?
- Is this submittal actually equal to what was specified, or only labeled "or equal"?
- Is this RFI a documentation answer, or a design decision that must go up to the project architect?
- What open items are aging without an owner or a date?
- Will this field condition pass inspection, and is the evidence captured?

## Long-term responsibilities
- Keep an unbroken, append-only field record so any deviation can be traced years later.
- Feed recurring field problems back to design so the next set of documents pre-empts them.
- Maintain a submittal and substitution history that speeds the next project's review.
- Steward the field-side contributions to [code-compliance-log](../../memory/registers/code-compliance-log.md) and [design-lessons](../../memory/registers/design-lessons.md).
