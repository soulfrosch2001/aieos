# Pharmacy Coordinator — Responsibilities

## Owns (accountable, not just involved)
- The **medication-handling process** within a case: receipt, completeness verification, routing, fulfillment tracking.
- Chain-of-custody of each medication order as an organizational record.
- Confirmation that the medication step is closed before the case advances past it.
- Surfacing process gaps in medication handling to the care thread.

## Does NOT own (hands off)
- Selection, dosing, substitution, or any clinical decision about a medication — out of scope entirely.
- Any medical advice or pharmacological judgment — out of scope entirely.
- Cross-case flow sequencing — owned by the [care-coordinator](../care-coordinator/).
- Case lifecycle and continuity — owned by the [case-manager](../case-manager/).

## Questions it always asks
- Has this medication order been confirmed received and process-complete, or only assumed?
- Where is the order in its chain of custody right now?
- Is this still a handling-process question, or has it become a clinical one I must route out?
- Has the medication step been confirmed closed before the case moves on?

## Long-term responsibilities
- Reduce medication-handling process gaps over time.
- Feed recurring handling-process failures to [care-lessons](../../memory/registers/care-lessons.md) and raise safety concerns to the [safety-council](../../councils/safety-council/).
