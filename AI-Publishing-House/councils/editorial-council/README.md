# Editorial Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: editorial-director
Extends: architecture-council

## Purpose
The room that adjudicates **editorial direction and standards** — the cross-cutting
questions no single editor owns alone: what the house bar *is*, how a difficult
manuscript should be shaped, and which standard wins when two are in tension. It
inherits the stdlib [architecture-council](../../../councils/architecture-council/):
editorial standards are the house's "architecture", and the question is the same —
*is this the right shape for the work, and what are we deliberately trading away?*

## Out of scope
- Whether to acquire a title — that is the
  [acquisition-council](../acquisition-council/).
- Release date and channel sequencing — that is the
  [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/).
- Doing the edit — a council **decides, it never builds** (Directive
  [#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)). The
  [developmental-editor](../../03-editorial/developmental-editor/) executes the
  ruling.

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [editorial-director](../../01-executive/editorial-director/)
  — owns editorial standards and holds the editorial veto on work below standard
  ([decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md)).
- **Core** (must attend): [developmental-editor](../../03-editorial/developmental-editor/),
  [copy-editor](../../03-editorial/copy-editor/),
  [proofreader](../../03-editorial/proofreader/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [acquisitions-editor](../../02-acquisitions/acquisitions-editor/)
  (intent of the book as acquired), [book-designer](../../04-production/book-designer/)
  (where text decisions hit the page), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/)
  (quality/process veto, never directs).

## When convened
At [T3](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) — a standard-defining or
cross-title editorial question — needing council **plus** executive sign-off. A
single-book direction call may convene at [T2](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).
Not standing; one question, then disbanded (Directive
[#6](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Inputs
The manuscript or standard in question, at least two viable editorial directions
with trade-offs, the affected house standards, the blast radius across the list,
and any relevant prior rulings from
[editorial-standards](../../memory/registers/editorial-standards.md).
