# 02-Acquisitions
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: acquisitions-editor
Extends: none

## Mission
Acquisitions finds, evaluates, and signs the books the house will publish. It is the front door of the list: it sources manuscripts and proposals, judges fit against house direction, negotiates the terms that make a book ours to publish, and hands a clean, contracted project to Editorial. The department is accountable for the quality and viability of what enters the pipeline — not for direction (that is the [CEO](../01-executive/ceo/)), and not for editorial standards on the page (that is the [Editorial Director](../01-executive/editorial-director/)).

## Lead
[acquisitions-editor](acquisitions-editor/) — accountable for the department's output and quality: which books are recommended for the list and the soundness of the case behind each.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [acquisitions-editor](acquisitions-editor/) | Judges fit and builds the acquisition case; recommends what the house signs. |
| [literary-scout](literary-scout/) | Sources and surfaces manuscripts and proposals worth the editor's attention. |
| [contracts-manager](contracts-manager/) | Turns a "yes" into clean, defensible terms the house can publish on. |
