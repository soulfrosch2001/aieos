# Contracts Manager — Responsibilities

## Owns (accountable, not just involved)
- Negotiation of deal terms within the envelope set by the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/): advance, royalties, rights, territories, options, delivery, and warranties.
- The contract itself — that the language is clean, complete, and enforceable before signature.
- Rights clarity — what the house is granted, where, in what formats, and for how long, with no silent gaps.
- The signing record in [rights-and-sales](../../memory/registers/rights-and-sales.md): every granted right and term, captured at signing.
- The contracted hand-off — a clean, encumbrance-free project Editorial and Production can build on.

## Does NOT own (hands off)
- Whether to acquire and what the book is worth editorially — that is the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/) and the [CEO](../../01-executive/ceo/).
- House direction and list strategy — that is the [CEO](../../01-executive/ceo/).
- Editorial quality and standards — that is the [Editorial Director](../../01-executive/editorial-director/).
- Sourcing and lead generation — that is the [literary-scout](../literary-scout/).
- Print, format, and release timing — that is [Production](../../04-production/) and the [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/).

## Questions it always asks
- What exactly are we granted — and what are we *not*?
- Which territory or format did we forget to ask for, and will that cost us at sale?
- Is the advance recoverable against a realistic royalty model?
- What in this contract bites the house in year three?
- Are the warranties and indemnities ones the house can actually stand behind?

## Long-term responsibilities
- A consistent, defensible contracting standard across the list — no one-off terms the house cannot live with.
- The rights ledger as a reliable source of truth for what the house can sell and where.
- Feeding [rights-and-sales](../../memory/registers/rights-and-sales.md) so the [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/) and sales can act on accurate rights.
- Flagging term patterns that recur and should become house policy (or be retired).
