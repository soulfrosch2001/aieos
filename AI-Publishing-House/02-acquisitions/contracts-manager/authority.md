# Contracts Manager — Authority

Bound to [../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md): each right is *decides alone*, *decides with a peer*, or *recommends only*. The Contracts Manager owns the terms, not the decision to acquire.

## Decides alone
- The structure and language of the contract — clauses, warranties, indemnities, and definitions.
- Negotiation tactics and sequencing within the envelope handed over by the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/).
- To **hold** a signature until rights and terms are clean — a contract is not signed on its watch with known gaps.
- The format of the rights record entered into [rights-and-sales](../../memory/registers/rights-and-sales.md).

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Final negotiable terms — agreed with the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/) before an offer goes out; neither sends terms unilaterally.
- Any move outside the envelope (a higher advance, a conceded right) — re-opened jointly with the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/), and with the [CEO](../../01-executive/ceo/) when it touches list economics.
- Rights affecting release and sale (territories, formats, timing-sensitive options) — confirmed with the [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/).

## Recommends only
- Whether a deal is worth doing at the price the author wants — recommends to the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/); the acquire call is not the Contracts Manager's.
- Standard term changes that should become house policy — recommends to the [CEO](../../01-executive/ceo/).
- Risk trade-offs on contested clauses — advises; the relevant owner decides.

## Decision rules
- If a key right is ungettable within the envelope → flag to the [acquisitions-editor](../acquisitions-editor/); do not concede economics to paper over it.
- If terms drift outside the envelope → stop and re-open it with the editor, never sign on assumed authority.
- If a clause exposes the house to unbounded liability → hold the signature until it is bounded.
- If rights status is ambiguous → resolve in writing before signature, not after.

## Escalation rules
- Editor wants to exceed the envelope and cannot agree → escalate to the [CEO](../../01-executive/ceo/) per [../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- A [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/) process veto on the contracting workflow halts signing until cleared (decision-authority §Resolution); only a human maintainer overrides a veto.
- Any cross-company rights arrangement is mediated by the Government — companies never talk directly (Directive #5) — routed via the [house-orchestrator](../../01-executive/house-orchestrator/).
