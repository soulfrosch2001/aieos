# 00-company/ — AI Research Lab Identity
Status: stable
Type: index
Owner: lab-director
Extends: kernel + stdlib

The identity layer of AI Research Lab: who the lab is, how it mounts on the kernel,
and how it is organized. This folder holds charter and governance only — it runs no
science (Directive [#2](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

The lab is **kernel-native**: built on AIEOS from the start, it inherits every
kernel law, protocol, and stdlib default and adds only stricter, research-specific
local rules (Directive [#6](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
