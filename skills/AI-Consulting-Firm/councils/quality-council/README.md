# Quality Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: chief-auditor
Extends: architecture-council

## Purpose

Adjudicates analytical quality and methodology across engagements. The council judges whether the work meets the firm's analytical bar — sound data, defensible logic, traceable assumptions, methods fit for the question — independent of whether the conclusion is convenient. It is the firm's conscience on rigor.

**Out of scope:** it does not author analysis, choose strategy, or direct delivery; the chair never delivers client work and never directs (Directive [#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)). It rules on quality; it does not do the work it judges.

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks, holds the quality veto): [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md).
- **Core** (must attend): [engagement-director](../../01-executive/engagement-director/README.md) (methodology veto), [data-analyst](../../02-diagnosis/data-analyst/README.md), [research-lead](../../02-diagnosis/research-lead/README.md).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [financial-modeler](../../03-strategy/financial-modeler/README.md), [strategy-consultant](../../03-strategy/strategy-consultant/README.md), [implementation-lead](../../04-implementation/implementation-lead/README.md).

## When convened

Not standing. Convened by tier — see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). **T2** work gets a quality sign-off; **T3+** strategic work requires it before delivery. Also convened on demand whenever the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md) flags a quality concern. Routed and sized by the [engagement-orchestrator](../../01-executive/engagement-orchestrator/README.md) ([orchestration.md](../../../kernel/protocols/orchestration.md)).

## Inputs

The analysis under review with its data sources and methods, the assumption log, the model or evidence base, prior related entries in the [engagement-decisions](../../memory/registers/engagement-decisions.md) register, and open items in the [risk-register](../../memory/registers/risk-register.md).
