# Engagement Council — Process

## Discussion rules
Follows the kernel debate norms in [communication.md](../../../kernel/protocols/communication.md). Evidence beats seniority; the diagnosis chain is challenged openly. Dissent is recorded, never suppressed (Directive [#8](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)). The chair speaks last on contested points to avoid anchoring. Implementation reality (raised by the [implementation-lead](../../04-implementation/implementation-lead/README.md)) is weighed as heavily as strategic elegance.

## Decision process
1. The [strategy-consultant](../../03-strategy/strategy-consultant/README.md) presents the recommendation and the diagnosis-to-strategy chain.
2. Core members test it: evidence (business-analyst), feasibility and adoption (implementation-lead), rigor and methodology (chair and chief-auditor), economics (financial-modeler, if present).
3. The council names remaining concerns, risks, and the alternatives considered.
4. The chair calls for consensus; on a contested point, the chair decides and the dissent is recorded.
5. Verdict: **issue**, **revise and re-review**, or **do not issue**. Any [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md) quality veto or [engagement-director](../../01-executive/engagement-director/README.md) methodology veto halts issuance regardless of vote ([decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md)).

## Approval gate by tier
- **Approves alone:** T0–T2 — the council's sign-off is sufficient to issue.
- **Must escalate:** T3–T4 — council plus the [managing-partner](../../01-executive/managing-partner/README.md); deadlocks escalate per [escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).

## Escalation
Deadlocks the chair cannot resolve, or vetoes in dispute, escalate one level up to the [managing-partner](../../01-executive/managing-partner/README.md); a quality veto is overridden only by a human maintainer ([decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md); [escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md)). Cross-company matters route through the Government (Directive [#4](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
