# Engagement Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: engagement-director
Extends: feature-council

## Purpose

Reviews engagement strategy before any recommendation issues to the client. The council pressure-tests the chain — does the recommendation follow from the diagnosis, is it the right answer for *this* client, and can it actually be implemented and adopted. It decides whether the engagement's strategy is sound enough to put in front of the client.

**Out of scope:** it does not author the strategy, run the analysis, or manage delivery; it does not decide whether the firm took the engagement (that is the [managing-partner](../../01-executive/managing-partner/README.md)). It reviews; it does not deliver (Directive [#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [engagement-director](../../01-executive/engagement-director/README.md) (CTO + methodology veto).
- **Core** (must attend): [strategy-consultant](../../03-strategy/strategy-consultant/README.md), [implementation-lead](../../04-implementation/implementation-lead/README.md), [business-analyst](../../02-diagnosis/business-analyst/README.md), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [financial-modeler](../../03-strategy/financial-modeler/README.md), [change-manager](../../04-implementation/change-manager/README.md), [operations-partner](../../01-executive/operations-partner/README.md).

## When convened

Not standing. Convened by tier — see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). **T2** engagements require its sign-off before recommendations issue; **T3+** require it plus the [managing-partner](../../01-executive/managing-partner/README.md). Routed and sized by the [engagement-orchestrator](../../01-executive/engagement-orchestrator/README.md) ([orchestration.md](../../../kernel/protocols/orchestration.md)).

## Inputs

The diagnosis and its evidence, the proposed strategy with its rationale and assumptions, the financial case, an implementation feasibility read, and the relevant open items in the [risk-register](../../memory/registers/risk-register.md).
