# Risk Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: chief-compliance-auditor
Extends: security-council

## Purpose
Reviews the legal and conflict risk on a matter: conflicts of interest, information-wall integrity, regulatory boundary exposure, confidentiality and privilege risk, and ethical fitness. It decides whether the firm's risk posture on a matter is acceptable, acceptable-with-controls, or unacceptable. It does **not** decide the matter's substantive legal strategy (that is the [matter-review-council](../matter-review-council/)) and it does not author advice (Prime Directive #2). This is the council closest to the Chief Auditor's absolute veto.

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [chief-compliance-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-compliance-auditor/) — holder of the absolute ethics/compliance veto.
- **Core** (must attend): [compliance-counsel](../../04-compliance/compliance-counsel/); [general-counsel](../../01-executive/general-counsel/); the matter's lead attorney.
- **Advisory** (as needed): [regulatory-analyst](../../04-compliance/regulatory-analyst/), [operations-partner](../../01-executive/operations-partner/) (for control-burden feasibility).

## When convened
By tier trigger, not standing — see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). Convenes when conflict or regulatory exposure is flagged (T2+) and mandatorily before any high-tier matter (T3+) issues advice.

## Inputs
- The matter and its proposed strategy.
- The conflict and information-wall check from [compliance-counsel](../../04-compliance/compliance-counsel/).
- Current regulatory readings and exposure from the [regulatory-analyst](../../04-compliance/regulatory-analyst/).
- Open entries in the [risk-register](../../memory/registers/risk-register.md).
