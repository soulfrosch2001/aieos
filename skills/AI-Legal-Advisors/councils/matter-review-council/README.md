# Matter Review Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: general-counsel
Extends: feature-council

## Purpose
Reviews a matter's strategy before advice issues — the theory of the matter, the supporting authority, the risks, and the consistency with the firm's prior positions. It decides whether the strategy is sound enough to proceed to client advice. It does **not** decide whether to take the matter (that is the [managing-partner](../../01-executive/managing-partner/)), how to staff or sequence it (the [operations-partner](../../01-executive/operations-partner/)), or whether it clears ethics (the [risk-council](../risk-council/) and the Chief Auditor's veto). It reviews substance, not delivery.

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [general-counsel](../../01-executive/general-counsel/) — owner of legal soundness and cross-matter consistency.
- **Core** (must attend): the matter's lead attorney; [corporate-counsel](../../02-advisory/corporate-counsel/) or [litigator](../../03-litigation/litigator/) as the practice dictates; [compliance-counsel](../../04-compliance/compliance-counsel/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [regulatory-analyst](../../04-compliance/regulatory-analyst/), [legal-researcher](../../03-litigation/legal-researcher/), [ip-attorney](../../02-advisory/ip-attorney/).

## When convened
By tier trigger, not standing — see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). Convenes for substantive matters reaching strategy sign-off (T2+); mandatory before a formal [legal-opinion](../../workflows/legal-opinion.md) issues (T3+).

## Inputs
- The proposed matter strategy and the authority it rests on.
- Current regulatory readings from the [regulatory-analyst](../../04-compliance/regulatory-analyst/).
- Relevant prior positions from the [precedent](../../memory/registers/precedent.md) register.
- Any open risk flags from the [risk-register](../../memory/registers/risk-register.md).
