# 02-Advisory
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: corporate-counsel
Extends: none

## Mission
The Advisory department advises clients on transactions and structure: entity formation, corporate governance, M&A and financings, commercial contracts, and intellectual property strategy. It produces forward-looking, transaction-shaping counsel — the advice clients act on *before* a deal closes or a structure is set — as distinct from the after-the-fact dispute work of [03-litigation](../03-litigation/) and the rule-checking discipline of [04-compliance](../04-compliance/). Its output is held to the firm's legal-soundness bar set by the [general-counsel](../01-executive/general-counsel/).

## Lead
[corporate-counsel](corporate-counsel/) — accountable for the department's output and quality, transactional coherence across matters, and the structural integrity of the advice the firm signs.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [corporate-counsel](corporate-counsel/) | Advises on entity structure, governance, M&A and financings; owns transaction architecture. |
| [contract-attorney](contract-attorney/) | Drafts and negotiates the operative agreements; owns the words that bind. |
| [ip-attorney](ip-attorney/) | Advises on IP ownership, protection, and transfer; owns the intangible-asset layer of every deal. |

## Councils it sits on
- [matter-review-council](../councils/matter-review-council/) — advisory matters are reviewed here before sign-off.
- [risk-council](../councils/risk-council/) — surfaces transaction and IP risk for the auditor's lens.

## Memory it feeds
- [precedent](../memory/registers/precedent.md) — structural decisions, deal terms, and standard positions worth reusing.
- [matter-log](../memory/registers/matter-log.md) — what each advisory matter taught the firm.
- [risk-register](../memory/registers/risk-register.md) — transaction and IP risks raised for tracking.
