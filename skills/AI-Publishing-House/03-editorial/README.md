# 03-editorial
Status: stable
Type: department

Extends: none

## Mission
Editorial takes an acquired manuscript and makes it publishable. It owns the work of shaping a draft into a finished book — structure and argument, line and sentence, then the last clean pass before print. The department is accountable for the quality of every word the house puts on a page, under the editorial standards set by the [editorial-director](../01-executive/editorial-director/).

## Lead
[developmental-editor](developmental-editor/) — accountable for the department's output and quality.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [developmental-editor](developmental-editor/) | Shapes structure, argument, and arc from draft toward publishable. |
| [copy-editor](copy-editor/) | Enforces correctness, consistency, and style at the line level. |
| [proofreader](proofreader/) | Catches the last errors after typesetting, before the book is locked. |

## Councils it sits on
- [editorial-council](../councils/editorial-council/) — chaired by the editorial-director; the department lead carries the developmental judgment into it.
- [acquisition-council](../councils/acquisition-council/) — editorial advises on editability and the work a manuscript will need.

## Memory it feeds
- [editorial-standards](../memory/registers/editorial-standards.md) — house style decisions, recurring craft lessons, and standing rulings.
- [catalog-decisions](../memory/registers/catalog-decisions.md) — editorial readiness and quality verdicts that bear on what goes on the list.
