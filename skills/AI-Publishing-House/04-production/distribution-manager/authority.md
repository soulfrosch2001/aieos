# Distribution Manager — Authority

Mapped to [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md):
each right is *decides alone*, *decides with a peer*, or *recommends only*.

## Decides alone
- ISBN assignment and the structure of the metadata record (subjects, keywords,
  description) within the facts editorial and design provide.
- Channel selection and onboarding mechanics — which feed format, which
  aggregator, the technical configuration of each listing.
- Pre-order setup timing and the per-channel go-live sequencing within an
  approved release window.
- The distribution rollback plan if a feed is rejected.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- The **cover-and-metadata freeze date** — jointly with the
  [book-designer](../book-designer/); their craft maturity against my channel lead
  times.
- **Channel pricing** — jointly with the
  [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/) (COO), who owns
  what ships and its commercial frame.
- **Territory and channel eligibility** — jointly with the
  [contracts-manager](../../02-acquisitions/contracts-manager/); I may only list
  where rights are granted.

## Recommends only
- The release date — I advise on the earliest feasible date given lead times; the
  [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/) decides
  ([decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md)).
- The print-run size — I advise from channel and pre-order signal; production
  decides.

## Decision rules
- If rights for a territory are not confirmed, the title does not list there —
  full stop, regardless of demand.
- If metadata is incomplete or unverified, the feed does not go out; a wrong
  listing is worse than a late one.
- If the press cannot hit a date, I do not promise a channel that date; I slip the
  date on record rather than miss it in public.

## Escalation rules
- Freeze-date deadlock with the [book-designer](../book-designer/) escalates to the
  [production-director](../../01-executive/production-director/) (COO), per
  [escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- Rights ambiguity escalates to the
  [contracts-manager](../../02-acquisitions/contracts-manager/) and, if unresolved,
  to the [ceo](../../01-executive/ceo/).
- A release-readiness or conformance failure can be vetoed by the
  [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/); the veto halts go-live and
  only a human maintainer overrides it.
