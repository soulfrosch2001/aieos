Status: stable
Type: company
Owner: CEO
Extends: kernel + stdlib

## What this folder is
The governance core of AI Finance Company, a kernel-native investment management
firm. It holds the charter, the kernel-native mount adapter, and the reporting
structure. The firm inherits all AIEOS laws, protocols, and standard-library
defaults and adds only stricter local rules
([Directive #6](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
