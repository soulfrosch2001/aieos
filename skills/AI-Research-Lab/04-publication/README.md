# Publication

Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: science-writer
Extends: none

## Mission
Publication is the lab's last mile: it turns validated findings into honest,
legible, defensible public artifacts — papers, preprints, datasets, and
disclosures — and it guards the ethics of everything that leaves the building.
A result that cannot be communicated clearly and cannot stand up to scrutiny has
not been finished. This department exists so that the lab's science reaches the
world as *truth that holds*, not hype that crumbles.

## What this department owns
- The narrative and prose of every external artifact (papers, abstracts,
  preprints, plain-language summaries).
- Reproducibility-of-the-record: that what is claimed matches what was done and
  what the data show.
- Research ethics and risk review for anything intended for release — human/animal
  subjects, dual-use, consent, data privacy, conflict-of-interest disclosure.
- The publication gate: the final ethics and communication sign-off before
  anything is submitted or posted.

## What it does NOT own
- The science itself — methods and statistical validity belong to
  [02-research/](../02-research/README.md) and [03-analysis/](../03-analysis/README.md).
- Scientific-rigor veto — that is the [research-director](../01-executive/research-director/README.md).
- Routing and tier sizing — that is the [lab-orchestrator](../01-executive/lab-orchestrator/README.md)
  (Directive [#2](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md), the orchestrator routes, never builds).

## Agents
- [science-writer](science-writer/README.md) — department lead; owns the prose,
  the narrative integrity, and the publication workflow.
- [ethics-officer](ethics-officer/README.md) — owns research ethics and the
  ethics gate; chairs the [ethics-council](../councils/ethics-council/README.md).

## How work reaches us
Work arrives sized to a tier — see
[engagement-tiers.md](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). Most publication
work is **T2** (a paper with real communication and ethics choices); a
high-stakes or dual-use disclosure escalates to **T3**. We run the
[publication workflow](../workflows/publication.md) and feed minutes and decisions
into the lab's [memory registers](../memory/README.md).

## Councils we drive
- [ethics-council](../councils/ethics-council/README.md) — chaired by the
  ethics-officer; the ethics-and-risk gate before release.
- We also send a seat to the [review-council](../councils/review-council/README.md)
  for clarity and claim-accuracy review.
