# Review Council — Process

## Discussion rules
Debate follows the kernel discussion norms — see
[orchestration.md](../../../kernel/protocols/orchestration.md) and
[communication.md](../../../kernel/protocols/communication.md). Critique attacks
the method and the evidence, never the person. **Dissent is recorded, never
suppressed** — a registered objection survives into the minutes even when the
chair overrules it. The burden of proof is on the claim: a finding is presumed
unproven until the evidence is shown.

## Decision process
1. The [principal-investigator](../../02-research/principal-investigator/README.md)
   presents the question, protocol, and headline findings.
2. The [statistician](../../03-analysis/statistician/README.md) walks the analysis;
   the [replication-specialist](../../03-analysis/replication-specialist/README.md)
   reports reproduction status.
3. The council probes for methodological flaws, p-hacking, unsupported claims, and
   confounds; each concern is logged.
4. Findings are graded: **sound as stated** / **sound with revisions** /
   **not yet sound** (rework required).
5. The chair ([research-director](../../01-executive/research-director/README.md))
   seeks consensus; on deadlock the chair decides, and the rigor veto is absolute
   on soundness. A [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md)
   quality veto stops the work.
6. Minutes, the grade, owners, and dissent are recorded — see [output.md](output.md).

## Approval gate by tier
- Approves alone: **T2** (council sign-off) per
  [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).
- Must escalate: **T3+** — needs council **+ executive** sign-off; route via
  [escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).

## Escalation
Soundness deadlocks resolve at the chair's rigor veto. A chief-auditor veto, or any
T3+ decision, escalates one level up the chain per
[decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md); only a human
maintainer overrides an auditor veto.
