# Performance Department
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: performance-marketer
Extends: none

## Mission
The Performance department is where intent meets measurement. It buys, optimizes,
and measures paid and organic performance across every client engagement —
turning brand and content into demand, traffic, leads, and revenue that can be
attributed and defended. Where Strategy decides *who we are* and Content decides
*what we say*, Performance decides *what works, at what cost, and how we know*.

## Lead
- [performance-marketer](performance-marketer/) — department lead. Owns paid and
  organic performance end to end; signs off [T1](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md)
  work and represents the department in councils.

## Agents
- [performance-marketer](performance-marketer/) — buys and optimizes paid media,
  owns budget pacing, channel mix, attribution, and the experiment program.
- [seo-specialist](seo-specialist/) — owns organic search performance: technical
  SEO, on-page, content briefs for search intent, and durable ranking growth.

## How we work
- We inherit the [kernel laws](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md). We measure
  everything against the client's end user and their goal (Directive
  [#1](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- We do not route or self-assign: the
  [agency-orchestrator](../01-executive/agency-orchestrator/) sizes the tier and
  fans work out (Directives [#2](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md),
  [#4](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- Significant spend or strategy shifts are debated in the
  [campaign-council](../councils/campaign-council/) before launch (Directive
  [#3](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- We feed results back: every campaign closes into
  [campaign-results](../memory/registers/campaign-results.md) and audience signal
  into [audience-insights](../memory/registers/audience-insights.md) (Directive
  [#8](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Tier ownership
- [T0–T1](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md): handled inside the department;
  lead signs off.
- [T2+](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md): goes to council; the department
  executes the approved plan, never the debate.
