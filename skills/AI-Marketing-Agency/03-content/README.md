# 03-Content
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: content-strategist
Extends: none

## Mission
The Content department produces the words and creative that carry the strategy into the world. Strategy decides what to say and to whom; Content decides exactly how it is said — the narrative, the lines, the posts, the calendar — so that every client touchpoint sounds like one coherent, deliberate voice. We are accountable for the craft and consistency of all produced content, on-brief and on-brand, across every channel.

## Lead
[content-strategist](content-strategist/) — accountable for the department's output and quality. The content-strategist owns the editorial plan and the standard every piece is held to.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [content-strategist](content-strategist/) | Owns the content plan, editorial standard, and how strategy becomes a narrative. |
| [copywriter](copywriter/) | Writes the words — headlines, body, scripts — that make the strategy land. |
| [social-media-manager](social-media-manager/) | Adapts content per platform, owns the calendar, and runs community presence. |

## Councils it sits on
- [campaign-council](../councils/campaign-council/) — content shapes what each campaign actually delivers.
- [brand-council](../councils/brand-council/) — content voice and tone are ratified here.

## Memory it feeds
- [brand-decisions](../memory/registers/brand-decisions.md) — voice, tone, and messaging rulings that become binding.
- [campaign-results](../memory/registers/campaign-results.md) — what content performed and why.
- [audience-insights](../memory/registers/audience-insights.md) — what resonance and language patterns we learned.
