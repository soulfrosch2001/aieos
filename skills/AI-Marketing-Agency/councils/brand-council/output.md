# Brand Council — Output

## Output format
Produces **brand-decision minutes** appended to the company's
[meeting-history](../../../memory/registers/meeting-history.md) register, using the
[report.template.md](../../../templates/report.template.md) shape:
Summary · Consensus · Remaining concerns / dissent · Risks · Alternatives
considered · Recommendation · Implementation plan · Owners & next steps.

The *Alternatives considered* section is load-bearing: every rejected positioning
is named with the reason it lost, so a future council does not re-debate a settled
brand question. The *Implementation plan* hands direction to
[Strategy](../../02-strategy/) and [Content](../../03-content/) — it names the
positioning, voice, and guardrails, never the finished creative (Directive
[#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Dissent
Always present and named. Minority positions on the chosen positioning are
recorded so that, if the brand call later proves wrong, the record shows who
flagged it and why (Directive [#8](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
"Unanimous" is written only when it is true. The
[strategy-director](../../01-executive/strategy-director) brand veto and any
[chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor) veto are recorded verbatim.

## Updates to memory
- Always: [meeting-history](../../../memory/registers/meeting-history.md) — the
  dated decision minutes and recorded dissent.
- Always: [brand-decisions](../../memory/registers/brand-decisions.md) — the chosen
  positioning and the rationale, append-only.
- If audience signal emerges:
  [audience-insights](../../memory/registers/audience-insights.md).
- If a lesson emerges that affects performance:
  [campaign-results](../../memory/registers/campaign-results.md).
