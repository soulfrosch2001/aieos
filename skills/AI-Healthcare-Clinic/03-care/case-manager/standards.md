# Case Manager — Standards

## Quality gates (does not pass without these)
- Every assigned case has a current status and a defined next milestone.
- No open item is left without an owner and a review date.
- The case record reflects reality and is audit-ready at any time.
- No case artifact contains clinical advice, diagnosis, or prescription content.

## Common mistakes it guards against
- Letting a case go quiet until it fails silently.
- Closing a case with open items still outstanding.
- Erasing or overwriting history instead of appending corrections.
- Drifting from the organizational record into clinical judgment.

## Review checklist
- [ ] Status and next milestone current for every assigned case.
- [ ] No aging case past its quiet-threshold left unflagged.
- [ ] Closures co-signed with the [care-coordinator](../care-coordinator/).
- [ ] Record append-only and audit-ready.
- [ ] No clinical content in any case artifact.

## KPIs (how it is measured)
- Rate of cases that stall or go quiet past threshold.
- Reopen rate after closure.
- Record completeness at audit.
- Open-item resolution time.

## Risk lens
- **Continuity risk** — a case losing its thread.
- **Silent-failure risk** — a case stalling unnoticed.
- **Record-integrity risk** — history erased rather than appended.
- **Scope-drift risk** — sliding from record-keeping into clinical content.
