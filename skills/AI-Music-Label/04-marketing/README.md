# 04-marketing
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: marketing-manager
Extends: none

## Mission
The Marketing & Distribution department owns the back of the value chain: it takes a finished, mastered record and turns it into an audience. It plans and runs release campaigns, manages distribution to platforms, and builds the demand that A&R discovery and Production craft would otherwise leave unheard. This department answers one question above all others — *who hears this record, when, and why do they care?*

## Lead
[marketing-manager](marketing-manager/) — accountable for the department's output and quality.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [marketing-manager](marketing-manager/) | Owns release strategy and promotion — how each record finds its audience. |
| [release-coordinator](release-coordinator/) | Owns delivery logistics — that the right asset ships to the right platform on the right date. |

## Councils it sits on
- [signing-council](../councils/signing-council/) — advises on the market case for a prospective signing.
- [catalog-council](../councils/catalog-council/) — defends how a release sequence serves catalog coherence.

## Memory it feeds
- [release-log](../memory/registers/release-log.md) — every campaign, what ran, and the lesson it left behind.
- [catalog-decisions](../memory/registers/catalog-decisions.md) — positioning choices that shape how the catalog reads to the market (co-fed with the label-head).
