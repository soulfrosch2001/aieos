# Greenlight Council — Process

## Discussion rules
Follows the studio debate norms in
[communication.md](../../../kernel/protocols/communication.md). One idea is
presented and challenged on its merits, not its champion. Dissent is recorded,
never suppressed — a quiet "no" in the room must appear in the minutes. The
[creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/README.md)'s creative veto
and the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md)'s process veto
are surfaced before the vote, not after.

## Decision process
1. The [screenwriter](../../02-development/screenwriter/README.md) presents the
   proposal: logline, treatment, audience, and the bet.
2. The [line-producer](../../01-executive/line-producer/README.md) states the
   budget envelope and whether the studio has capacity now.
3. The creative-director assesses fit with studio vision and raises any veto.
4. Advisory voices (auditor on process, vfx-supervisor on feasibility) report.
5. The council compares at least two framings of the bet and names what is being
   traded away.
6. The [ceo](../../01-executive/ceo/README.md) calls the decision: greenlight,
   pass, or develop further — and, on deadlock, decides as chair.

## Approval gate by tier
- Approves alone: [T0–T2](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) creative
  explorations and small development bets handled within development.
- Must escalate: [T3+](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) greenlights
  require this council **plus** CEO executive sign-off; studio-direction or
  cross-company implications escalate to the Government per
  [escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md) and
  [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md).

## Escalation
Deadlocks the chair cannot break, or decisions that bind the wider studio strategy,
escalate to the Government via the
[studio-orchestrator](../../01-executive/studio-orchestrator/README.md)
([escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md)). No company talks to
another directly (Directive [#4](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
