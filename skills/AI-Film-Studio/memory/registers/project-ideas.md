# Project Ideas
Status: stable
Type: memory-register
Owner: screenwriter
Extends: future-improvements

**Purpose:** the studio's parking lot of pitches and seeds — strong ideas surfaced
in development but not made this cycle, held so a future slate can draw on them
instead of starting cold.
**Discipline:** append-mostly — an idea is never erased; when it is made, killed, or
reframed, a new dated entry records the change and back-links the original.

## Schema
| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Date | When parked (YYYY-MM-DD). |
| Idea | Stable id, e.g. `IDEA-047`. |
| Title | Working title or one-line concept. |
| Pitch | The logline and why it could matter. |
| Origin | Where it came from (development pass, council, agent note). |
| Why parked | The reason it was not made now (band, timing, rights). |
| Status | `parked` · `in-development` · `made` · `dropped`. |

## Example entry
| 2026-06-26 | IDEA-047 | Saltlight | A lighthouse keeper outlives the coast town that needed her | [development](../../workflows/development.md) Gate B, lost to slate timing | Strong voice but no budget band this cycle | parked |

## Who updates this & when
The [screenwriter](../../02-development/screenwriter/) appends at Gate B of
[development](../../workflows/development.md) when a project is parked rather than
greenlit, and updates the status when a parked idea re-enters development. The
[development-executive](../../02-development/development-executive/) reviews the lot
when building a new slate (Directive [#6](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Flow
- Knowledge flows **down** from the parent register `future-improvements` to this
  studio register.
- Decisions of consequence flow **up**: an idea that rises to a studio-wide slate
  decision is promoted to the Enterprise.
See [../../../kernel/protocols/memory-flow.md](../../../kernel/protocols/memory-flow.md).
