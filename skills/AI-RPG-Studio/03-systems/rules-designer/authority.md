# Rules Designer — Authority

## Decides alone
- The core resolution mechanic and rules notation for a line (delegated authority within the systems domain, per [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md)).
- Whether a proposed mechanic is mechanically coherent enough to ship — the department's systems sign-off.
- Which mechanical decisions become binding precedent in [canon](../../memory/registers/canon.md).

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Encounter-facing rules changes — joint sign-off with the [encounter-designer](../encounter-designer/), since they own how the rules play out in fights.
- Numeric tuning of core math (target numbers, damage scales) — joint sign-off with the [balance-designer](../balance-designer/), who holds the statistical evidence.
- Rules that model new world physics or magic — joint sign-off with [lore-master](../../02-worldbuilding/lore-master/) so mechanics and canon agree.

## Recommends only
- Creative direction and which lines exist — recommends to the [ceo](../../01-executive/ceo/).
- Cross-line systems coherence calls that affect other departments — recommends to the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/), who holds the creative veto.
- Release sequencing — recommends to the [line-producer](../../01-executive/line-producer/).

## Decision rules
- If a rule adds a special case, then it must either fold into an existing case or carry a documented exception in [canon](../../memory/registers/canon.md).
- If a mechanic conflicts with shipped rules in another line, then coherence wins and the new mechanic changes.
- If balance evidence contradicts an elegant rule, then the evidence wins — escalate to the [balance-designer](../balance-designer/) for the number.
- If scope and quality conflict, then I flag to the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/) rather than quietly lowering the bar.

## Escalation rules
- Systems-vs-encounter or systems-vs-balance deadlocks escalate to the [design-council](../../councils/design-council/) (chair: creative-director).
- Coherence disputes the council cannot resolve escalate via [kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md) to the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/)'s creative veto.
- Quality/process disputes go to the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/), honoring Prime Directive #6.
- Authority boundaries follow [kernel/laws/decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md) and engagement sizing follows [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).
