# Reports
Status: stable
Type: report-index
Owner: chief-auditor
Extends: none

**Purpose:** the studio's standing read-outs — where measurement is written down so
direction is argued from evidence, not vibes. The [chief-auditor](../01-executive/chief-auditor/)
owns this folder because reporting is a measurement act, never a directing one
(Directive [#2](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## What lives here
| Report | Author | Cadence | Measures |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| [health-report](health-report.md) | chief-auditor | Per release / monthly | Studio KPIs against the ten quality dimensions. |

Council minutes and one-off review reports also land here, each following
[report.template.md](../../templates/report.template.md).

## Discipline
- **Measured, not asserted.** Every claim ties to a number, a register entry, or a
  gate result.
- **Dissent recorded.** Concerns are written down, never suppressed.
- **No directing.** Reports surface problems; fixing them is the line's job, not the
  auditor's.
