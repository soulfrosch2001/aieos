# Faction Designer — Authority

Authority maps to [kernel decision-authority](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md): every right below is *decides alone*, *decides with a peer*, or *recommends only*. The faction-designer may add stricter local rules over faction coherence but never loosens a [Kernel Law](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md).

## Decides alone
- A faction's present-day goals, strategy, leverage, and the conflicts it drives.
- Faction clocks — how groups act and escalate over time.
- Whether a faction is dramatically live enough to generate play, or is inert set dressing to be cut.
- The conflict map for a setting: who opposes whom and where the pressure points sit.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- A faction's historical identity and founding — with the [lore-master](../lore-master/), splitting live present from the dated past.
- Where faction borders and holdings fall — with the [cartographer](../cartographer/), reconciling political intent with what terrain permits.
- Mechanical force composition for a faction — with [03-systems](../../03-systems/).

## Recommends only
- Which factions a campaign should foreground — recommends to [04-narrative](../../04-narrative/) and the [line-producer](../../01-executive/line-producer/).
- Canon implications when a faction's action would change the setting — recommends to the [lore-master](../lore-master/).

## Decision rules
- If a faction's action would contradict established canon, then it routes to the lore-master before it becomes setting fact.
- If a faction wants territory terrain cannot support, then the claim is contested or reframed with the cartographer, not granted.
- If a faction produces no choice for players, then it is reworked until it does or it is cut.

## Escalation rules
- History-vs-present deadlock with the lore-master escalates to the lore-master as department lead, then to the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) (creative veto). See [kernel escalation protocol](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- A [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/) quality/process veto stops the work; only a human maintainer overrides it (Prime Directive #6).
