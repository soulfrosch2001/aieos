# Narrative

Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: campaign-writer
Extends: none

## Mission
The Narrative department turns the studio's worlds and systems into things players
actually live through: campaigns, quests, and adventure modules. Worldbuilding gives
us a place; Systems gives us the verbs; Narrative gives players a reason to care and
a path to walk. We are accountable for the felt experience at the table — the arc, the
choices, the payoffs — within the canon and rules handed down to us.

## Members
- [campaign-writer](campaign-writer/) — department lead; owns campaign arcs, adventures, and prose voice.
- [quest-designer](quest-designer/) — owns individual quest structure, objectives, branching, and rewards.

## Operating context
- We serve the player experience first (Directive #1) and never invent canon or
  rules; we petition [Worldbuilding](../02-worldbuilding/) and [Systems](../03-systems/) for those.
- T2+ narrative work is debated before it is written (Directive #3) — usually in the
  [design-council](../councils/design-council/) for arc/system-touching choices and the
  [lore-council](../councils/lore-council/) for anything that asserts new canon.
- We feed the [campaign-ideas](../memory/registers/campaign-ideas.md) register and
  consume the [canon](../memory/registers/canon.md) register as our source of truth.
