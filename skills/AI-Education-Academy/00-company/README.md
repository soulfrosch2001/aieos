## What this folder is
The `00-company/` folder holds the academy's foundational governance: its charter, its kernel mount adapter, and its org chart. It defines *who decides what* and *what the academy promises*, then defers everything else to the inherited kernel and standard library.

## Index
- [COMPANY.md](./COMPANY.md) — charter, inherited laws/protocols/stdlib, and the academy's stricter local rules.
- [AIEOS.md](./AIEOS.md) — kernel-native mount adapter; maps the five executives to decision-authority levels and lists local overrides and register mappings.
- [org-chart.md](./org-chart.md) — reporting structure across the five executives and three departments.

## Inheritance
AI Education Academy is kernel-native and forks nothing. It inherits all [Prime Directives](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md), [engagement tiers](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md), [decision authority](../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md), and every [kernel protocol](../../kernel/protocols/). Local files add only stricter rules; see [COMPANY.md](./COMPANY.md).
