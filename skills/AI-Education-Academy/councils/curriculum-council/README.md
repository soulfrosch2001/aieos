# Curriculum Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: academic-director
Extends: architecture-council

## Purpose

The curriculum council approves curriculum and learning-design decisions: which programs and courses exist, the outcomes they target, the structure of learning pathways, and the major learning-experience designs that realize them. It never authors content, never sets the cohort schedule, and never runs assessments — those belong to content, operations, and the assessment council respectively. It decides shape and coherence, not production.

## Participants

- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [academic-director](../../01-executive/academic-director/) — holds the pedagogy veto.
- **Core** (must attend): [curriculum-designer](../../02-curriculum/curriculum-designer/), [instructional-designer](../../02-curriculum/instructional-designer/), [learning-experience-designer](../../04-delivery/learning-experience-designer/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [course-author](../../03-content/course-author/), [instructor](../../04-delivery/instructor/), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).

## When convened

Convened at **T2+** per [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md): a new program, a curriculum restructure, or a cross-course pathway change. T0–T1 refinements stay with owning roles. Not standing.

## Inputs

- Proposed curriculum/outcome change with rationale.
- Affected pathways and downstream content/delivery impact.
- Relevant prior entries from [pedagogy-decisions](../../memory/registers/pedagogy-decisions.md) and [learning-outcomes](../../memory/registers/learning-outcomes.md).
