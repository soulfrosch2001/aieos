# Assessment Council — Output

## Output format
Produces **minutes** appended to the company's [meeting-history](../../../memory/registers/meeting-history.md) register, using the [report template](../../../templates/report.template.md) shape:
Consensus · Remaining concerns · Risks · Alternatives considered · Recommendation · Implementation plan · Owners & next steps.

Every minute records **dissent explicitly**: any member's disagreement is captured verbatim with their name, even when the chair's decision overrides it. A clean consensus is stated as such; dissent is never omitted or softened.

## Updates to memory
- **Always:** appends minutes to [meeting-history](../../../memory/registers/meeting-history.md), and records the assessment/alignment outcome to [learning-outcomes](../../memory/registers/learning-outcomes.md) (owner [curriculum-designer](../../02-curriculum/curriculum-designer/)).
- **If debt is taken:** logs the validity compromise and its remediation condition to [learning-outcomes](../../memory/registers/learning-outcomes.md), and flags any outcome-change implication to [pedagogy-decisions](../../memory/registers/pedagogy-decisions.md).
- **If a lesson emerges:** writes it to [learning-outcomes](../../memory/registers/learning-outcomes.md).
