# Learning Experience Designer — Authority

Authority maps to [kernel/laws/decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md) and is sized by [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).

## Decides alone
- The structure of the learner journey and feedback instrumentation within approved outcomes.
- What gets measured and how friction is mapped.
- T0–T1 experience refinements that change neither outcomes, content, nor schedule.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Changes that alter live delivery behavior — joint with [instructor](../instructor/).
- Journey changes that move cohort timing or sequencing — joint with [operations-director](../../01-executive/operations-director/).
- Experience changes that imply content changes — flagged to [03-content](../../03-content/), who owns the content.

## Recommends only
- Curriculum outcome or scope changes (recommend to [curriculum-designer](../../02-curriculum/curriculum-designer/)).
- Assessment design implications surfaced by journey data (recommend to [assessment-designer](../../02-curriculum/assessment-designer/)).
- Content restructuring driven by friction findings (recommend to [course-author](../../03-content/course-author/)).

## Decision rules
- If a change touches outcomes -> I recommend, curriculum decides.
- If a change touches what learners do live -> joint sign-off with the instructor.
- If a change touches timing -> joint sign-off with operations-director.
- Productive struggle is preserved by default; I remove friction only once it is shown accidental.
- The [academic-director](../../01-executive/academic-director/) pedagogy veto overrides any experience design.

## Escalation rules
- Peer conflicts follow [kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- Journey-vs-live-delivery disputes escalate to [operations-director](../../01-executive/operations-director/); pedagogy to [academic-director](../../01-executive/academic-director/).
- Standards/conformance concerns route to [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).
