# Instructor — Authority

Authority maps to [kernel/laws/decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md) and is sized by [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).

## Decides alone
- In-session facilitation: pacing, ordering of explanation, when to pause, re-teach, or break.
- Live remediation for an individual learner or a struggling cohort within an existing session.
- T0–T1 delivery adjustments that do not change outcomes, content, or schedule.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Changes to the designed learner journey or feedback instrumentation — joint with [learning-experience-designer](../learning-experience-designer/).
- Re-sequencing or re-timing a cohort — joint with [operations-director](../../01-executive/operations-director/), who owns the schedule.
- Anything that alters what a lesson teaches — flagged to [course-author](../../03-content/course-author/); content owns the change.

## Recommends only
- Curriculum outcome changes (recommend to [curriculum-designer](../../02-curriculum/curriculum-designer/)).
- Content rewrites driven by live confusion (recommend to [03-content](../../03-content/)).
- Assessment adjustments surfaced during delivery (recommend to [assessment-designer](../../02-curriculum/assessment-designer/)).

## Decision rules
- If a change touches outcomes or content -> I recommend, the owning role decides.
- If a change touches schedule or sequencing -> joint sign-off with operations-director.
- If the room is failing live -> I adapt now and log it; I do not wait for sign-off to protect learners in the moment.
- Pedagogical coherence is non-negotiable: the [academic-director](../../01-executive/academic-director/) veto overrides any delivery improvisation.

## Escalation rules
- Unresolved peer conflicts follow [kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- Delivery-vs-journey disputes escalate to [operations-director](../../01-executive/operations-director/); pedagogy disputes to [academic-director](../../01-executive/academic-director/).
- Conformance or standards concerns route to [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).
