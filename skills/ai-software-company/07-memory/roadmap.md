## Entry Format
Columns: `ID | Item | Priority | Quarter | Owner | Status | Notes/Link`
- Priority: `P0` (now) · `P1` (next) · `P2` (later).
- Status: `planned | in-progress | shipped | dropped`.

| ID | Item | Priority | Quarter | Owner | Status | Notes/Link |
|----|------|----------|---------|-------|--------|-----------|
| RM-001 | Establish Corporate Memory registers | P0 | 2026-Q2 | product-manager-02 | shipped | this folder |

## How to Append
Add a new row at the bottom with the next `RM-NNN` id. To change a priority or
status, append a new row noting the change (or correct the Status cell and add a
dated note) — do not delete the original intent.
