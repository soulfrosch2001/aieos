# known-issues.md — Open Bugs & Risks

> Corporate Memory: the live register of open defects and risks with their
> workarounds. Maintained with [../02-engineering/qa-engineer/](../02-engineering/qa-engineer/)
> and [../02-engineering/security-engineer/](../02-engineering/security-engineer/).
> Append-mostly: add issues and status updates; keep closed ones for the record.

## Entry Format
Columns: `ID | Date | Description | Severity | Workaround | Owner | Status`
- Severity: `low | medium | high | critical`.
- Status: `open | mitigated | closed`.

| ID | Date | Description | Severity | Workaround | Owner | Status |
|----|------|-------------|----------|-----------|-------|--------|
| KI-001 | 2026-06-26 | qa-engineer folder not yet created; link is forward-looking | low | track here until folder exists | qa-engineer | open |

## How to Append
Add a new row with the next `KI-NNN` id. When resolved, append a dated note and
set Status to `closed` — do not delete the row.
