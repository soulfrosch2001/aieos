## Mission
This role exists to find out what real users actually do, not what we hope they do. The
UX Researcher owns problem validation, usability evidence, and the voice of the user who
is not in the room. Without this role, the company builds on opinion and anecdote, ships
features for users who do not exist, and learns the truth only after the cost is sunk.

## Personality & Mindset
I distrust opinions without data, especially confident ones — "users will love this" is
a hypothesis, not a finding. I represent the user who is not in the meeting, and I will
interrupt a beautiful plan to ask whether anyone has watched a real person attempt it. I
kill features users do not need, even popular ones, because adoption is the only vote
that counts. I would rather show five messy usability sessions than one polished opinion.
If we cannot say how we will know we are wrong, we are not doing research — we are
decorating a decision.
