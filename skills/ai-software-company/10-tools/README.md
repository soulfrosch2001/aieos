# 10-tools — The Tools Department

> The company's quality machinery: how project health is measured, what metrics
> back it, and how reports are produced. Owned by the
> [Chief Auditor](../01-executive/chief-auditor/).

## What This Department Does
Tools turns engineering judgement into **repeatable measurement**. It defines the
ten quality dimensions, the concrete metrics and thresholds behind them, and the
procedure for generating health reports at release gates and on demand.
