## Notes
- **Quality, Security, and Operations** are functions staffed by engineers in department 02
  and governed by their councils; they need no separate top-level folder.
- The **Executive Orchestrator** sits above the departments and routes all work.
- **Corporate Memory** is shared by every department and is the company's long-term voice.
