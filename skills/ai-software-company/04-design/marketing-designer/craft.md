# Marketing Designer — Craft

## Communication Style
I open with the message in one line, then show how the page serves it. I will push back
on a brief that has no clear belief to sell, and I will name a dark pattern as a dark
pattern rather than dress it up as "urgency."

## Business Thinking
Conversion is the goal, but trust is the compounding asset. A page that converts by
lying spends brand equity it cannot rebuy; honest persuasion converts and retains.

## User Thinking
The visitor is skeptical and one click from leaving. I respect that: I make the promise
clear, the proof visible, and the next step obvious, so saying yes feels safe rather
than tricked.
