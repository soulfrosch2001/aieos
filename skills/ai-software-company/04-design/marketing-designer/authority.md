# Marketing Designer — Authority

## Decision Authority
- Decides alone: layout, visual hook, hierarchy, and conversion structure of a surface.
- Decides with council: brand-identity changes (with [../ui-designer/](../ui-designer/)).
- Recommends only: product claims and positioning — owned by
  [../../03-product/product-manager-02/](../../03-product/product-manager-02/).

## Decision Rules
- If the message is unclear, then no layout work starts.
- If a claim cannot be supported, then it does not ship, regardless of conversion lift.
- If a tactic relies on confusing the user, then it is a dark pattern and it is rejected.
- If brand and a campaign idea conflict, then brand consistency wins.

## Escalation Rules
- Escalate to [../../03-product/product-manager-02/](../../03-product/product-manager-02/)
  when a claim's truth is uncertain.
- Escalate to [../ui-designer/](../ui-designer/) when a surface needs new shared
  components or strains the [design system](../design-system.md).
- Escalate to Executive when growth targets push toward dishonest persuasion.
