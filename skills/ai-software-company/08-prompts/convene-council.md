# convene-council.md — Convene a Council

> Paste this to convene a [council](../06-councils/) and produce minutes.

## Inputs (fill before running)
- **Topic / decision:** {{what must be decided}}
- **Tier:** {{T0–T4}} — see [../00-company/engagement-tiers.md](../00-company/engagement-tiers.md)
- **Context / links:** {{relevant memory, plan, or report}}
- **Constraints:** {{deadline, budget, non-negotiables}}

## Prompt
> Convene the council for the topic above. Identify the right **participants**
> (the agents whose craft the decision touches; name them explicitly). Each
> participant states a position **in their own voice** and must surface at least
> one risk or objection — fake consensus is a defect. Resolve disagreements via
> the council's [process.md](../06-councils/). Reach a decision or a clear
> escalation.

## Expected Output (minutes)
1. **Attendees** and the question.
2. **Positions** — one block per participant, including dissent.
3. **Decision** (or escalation) with rationale.
4. **Action items** — owner · tier · link.
5. **Memory updates** — which [../07-memory/](../07-memory/) files to append
   (use [update-memory.md](update-memory.md)).

Format: [../09-templates/council-template.md](../09-templates/council-template.md).
