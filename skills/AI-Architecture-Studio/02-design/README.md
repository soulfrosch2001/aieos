## Mission
The Design department shapes the architectural design of every project the studio takes on: the parti, the spatial idea, the building's relationship to site and climate, and the visual and material language that makes a project recognisably ours. It is accountable for design coherence and quality at the level where intent is born — before engineering hardens it and before delivery schedules it. The department turns a brief and a site into a defensible architectural proposition.

## Lead
[lead-architect](lead-architect/) — accountable for the department's output and quality.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [lead-architect](lead-architect/) | Owns the design proposition end-to-end and integrates concept, sustainability, and technical reality into one coherent scheme. |
| [concept-architect](concept-architect/) | Generates and stress-tests the parti — the core idea, massing, and spatial sequence — before it calcifies. |
| [sustainability-designer](sustainability-designer/) | Embeds climate, energy, and material-life thinking into the design from the first sketch, not as a bolt-on. |
