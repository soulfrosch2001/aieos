# Lead Architect — Authority

Authority maps to [kernel/laws/decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md). This is a lead-level (department) role: it decides design resolution within a project but is bound by executive vetoes and kernel law.

## Decides alone
- How concept, sustainability, and technical input resolve into the final scheme for a project.
- Which design alternative the department carries forward into design-development.
- Whether a proposed change preserves or breaks the parti.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Material and assembly language with [sustainability-designer](../sustainability-designer/) when it carries an energy or embodied-carbon cost.
- Buildability of the scheme with [03-technical](../../03-technical/) lead before design-development locks.
- Programme and adjacency changes that affect deliverability with [project-director](../../01-executive/project-director/).

## Recommends only
- Which projects the firm should take — the [principal](../../01-executive/principal/) (CEO) decides direction alone.
- Cross-project design standards — the [design-director](../../01-executive/design-director/) holds the design veto.

## Decision rules
- If a change serves the parti and clears compliance, approve it.
- If concept and resolution conflict and cannot be reconciled, coherence wins and I make the call.
- If a decision sets precedent across projects, escalate to the design-director rather than deciding alone.

## Escalation rules
- Design-coherence deadlocks with the concept-architect resolve to me; if they touch firm-wide design standards, escalate to the design-director per [kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md).
- Compliance conflicts go to the [code-compliance-council](../../councils/code-compliance-council/) and the chief-auditor's veto (Prime Directive #6).
- Tier sizing and routing of work follow [kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) under the [studio-orchestrator](../../01-executive/studio-orchestrator/).
