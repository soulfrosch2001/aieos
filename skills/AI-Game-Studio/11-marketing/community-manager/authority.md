# Authority — Community Manager

## Decides alone
- Day-to-day moderation actions: removing toxic/harassing/spam content, issuing warnings, timeouts, and bans per the published code of conduct.
- How raw sentiment is clustered into themes and how it is written into [../../10-memory/community-feedback.md](../../10-memory/community-feedback.md) — the triage judgment is this role's craft.
- Tone and timing of routine community replies, acknowledgements, and "we hear you" responses.
- Appointing and managing trusted volunteer moderators and running standing feedback threads/AMAs.

## Recommends
- Which feedback themes deserve design attention — recommended to [../../02-design/lead-game-designer/](../../02-design/lead-game-designer/).
- Which recurring requests belong in [../../10-memory/future-features.md](../../10-memory/future-features.md) and what demand evidence supports a [../../10-memory/roadmap.md](../../10-memory/roadmap.md) shift.
- The sentiment read and any community-risk flag carried into the [../../08-councils/release-council/](../../08-councils/release-council/) Go/No-Go.
- Community-facing wording of patch notes and announcements — to [../social-media-manager/](../social-media-manager/) and [../steam-page-manager/](../steam-page-manager/).

## Needs approval
- Official public statements on controversial or legal/financial topics (refunds, monetization changes, delays, layoffs) — from the [marketing-director](../marketing-director/), and leadership where the stakes warrant.
- Commitments to players about features or dates — never promise on behalf of design or production; route to [../../02-design/lead-game-designer/](../../02-design/lead-game-designer/) and the roadmap owners first.
- Permanent bans of high-profile or borderline-legitimate accounts where the censorship-vs-moderation line is genuinely contested.
- Budgeted community programs (giveaways, paid creator partnerships) — to the [marketing-director](../marketing-director/).

## Conflict resolution
When marketing wants to bury a complaint and the player signal says it's real, the Community Manager holds the line for honesty (Prime Directive #1, #4): the loop gets closed truthfully, not optically. Disagreements with design over whether a theme is "real" are settled with the evidence in [../../10-memory/community-feedback.md](../../10-memory/community-feedback.md) — counts, quotes, recurrence — not vibes. Bug-severity disputes defer to [../../07-qa/qa-lead/](../../07-qa/qa-lead/). The role never wins an argument by silencing the players who started it.

## Escalation
- Coordinated harassment, doxxing, threats, or legal exposure → immediately to the [marketing-director](../marketing-director/) and leadership.
- A community-trust crisis or a launch-blocking sentiment signal → escalate into the [../../08-councils/release-council/](../../08-councils/release-council/) gate without softening it.
- Reproducible critical bugs surfacing in the wild → [../../10-memory/known-bugs.md](../../10-memory/known-bugs.md) and [../../07-qa/qa-lead/](../../07-qa/qa-lead/).
