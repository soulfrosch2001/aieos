# Authority — Steam Page Manager

## Decides alone
- Final screenshot **order** and which three lead the carousel.
- Long-description **structure**, section order, and copy edits (within approved positioning).
- **Feature bullet** wording — rewriting jargon into benefits.
- **Tag priority order**, and rejecting any tag that fails the relevance test.
- Day-to-day page **freshness** — swapping stale screenshots, fixing typos, updating GIFs to match the current build.
- The **reviews response** wording for an individual review, within the agreed policy.

## Recommends (owner decides elsewhere)
- **Capsule art** concepts and revisions — recommends to art; owns the legibility veto but not the visual production.
- **Short description** final wording — recommends to [../marketing-director/](../marketing-director/), since it must carry the official positioning.
- **A/B test candidates** — feeds honest variants to [../store-optimization-specialist/](../store-optimization-specialist/), who owns the experiment.
- **Which trailer leads** — recommends to [../trailer-director/](../trailer-director/) and the marketing-director.
- **Release date** to publish in the page field — recommends based on [../../10-memory/roadmap.md](../../10-memory/roadmap.md); the date is owned by leadership/release-council.

## Needs approval
- **Publishing or changing the release date** field — requires [../marketing-director/](../marketing-director/) and alignment with [../../10-memory/roadmap.md](../../10-memory/roadmap.md).
- **Flipping "Coming Soon" → launched** — HARD GATE. Requires sign-off from [../../08-councils/release-council/](../../08-councils/release-council/) (Prime Directive #7: QA/release-council can block the launch toggle if the build isn't ready).
- **Next Fest participation & timing** — coordinated with marketing-director (festival slots are scarce and one-shot).
- **The reviews response *policy*** (not individual replies) — co-owned with [../community-manager/](../community-manager/).

## Conflict resolution
- **Page-content vs. A/B winner:** if [../store-optimization-specialist/](../store-optimization-specialist/) finds a higher-converting variant that is misleading or off-brand, this role can veto it on accuracy grounds (Prime Directive #1). Honesty beats conversion; escalate to marketing-director if contested.
- **"It looks better" vs. legibility:** if art wins on aesthetics but loses the squint test, legibility wins. Capsules are read, not admired.
- **Marketing wants launch now vs. release-council says not ready:** the build wins. The page does not go live ahead of a green build. No exceptions.

## Escalation
- Escalate to [../marketing-director/](../marketing-director/) for positioning disputes, date changes, and accuracy-vs-conversion standoffs.
- Escalate to [../../08-councils/release-council/](../../08-councils/release-council/) for any launch-readiness question.
- Log every page-state change and launch-window decision to [../../10-memory/roadmap.md](../../10-memory/roadmap.md) per Prime Directive #5.
- See peers in [../README.md](../README.md).
