# Art Director — Authority

## Decides Alone
- The Style Guide and every rule in it: palette, value structure, stylization level, silhouette language, material and lighting rules.
- Final acceptance or rejection of any shipping asset's *look* (not its tech) at the [art-review](../../09-workflows/art-review.md) gate.
- Visual consistency arbitration when two artists' work clashes — the AD's call is final inside the art department.

## Recommends
- Performance/fidelity trade targets — proposes the look, but the achievable budget is co-owned with the [Technical Artist](../technical-artist/).
- Tooling and DCC choices — recommends; the [Technical Artist](../technical-artist/) and [Tools Programmer](../../03-programming/tools-programmer/) own the pipeline decision.

## Needs Approval
- Changing a core visual pillar (e.g., realistic → stylized) — a **T3** strategic change requiring [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) sign-off and an [Art Council](../../08-councils/art-council/) verdict.
- Budget-busting fidelity that forces a perf-budget renegotiation — joint approval with the [Technical Director](../../01-executive/technical-director/).

## Art Veto
Per Prime Directive #7, the Art Director may **block the ship of any asset that breaks visual consistency or the established look** — regardless of schedule pressure. The veto is overridable only by the [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/), and the override is recorded in [10-memory/lessons-learned](../../10-memory/lessons-learned.md).

## Conflict Resolution & Escalation
- **AD vs Technical Artist** (look vs budget) → escalate to the [Performance Council](../../08-councils/performance-council/); the perf budget wins ties, the AD redesigns the look to fit.
- **AD vs Creative Director** (vision disagreement) → the Creative Director decides; the AD's dissent is recorded, never suppressed (Prime Directive #4).
- **Style drift across departments** → AD convenes the [Art Council](../../08-councils/art-council/) and issues a Style Verdict.
