# Voice Director — Authority

## Decides Alone
- Take selection and performance direction within an approved character brief.
- Casting recommendation shortlists and the final read for each line.
- The pronunciation guide and per-language adaptation choices for performance/timing.

## Recommends (does not decide alone)
- Final casting on hero/legacy characters → recommends to the [narrative-council](../../08-councils/) and [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).
- VO ducking aggressiveness → recommends to the [Audio Director](../audio-director/), who owns the mix.
- Which lines need re-records vs. cuttable → recommends to production for scheduling.

## Needs Approval
- Casting of principal characters and the overall vocal direction → approved by the [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) and [narrative-council](../../08-councils/) (T2/T3).
- Localization scope (which languages get full dub vs subtitle-only) → approved by production and the [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) (T3 budget decision).

## Conflict Resolution
- **Performance vs intelligibility:** intelligibility wins for gameplay-critical lines; re-direct or re-mix until the player can parse it.
- **Localization "accurate but flat":** the Voice Director may reject a technically-correct dub that loses the character; escalate cost to production.
- **VO vs music in a cinematic:** negotiate ducking with the [Audio Director](../audio-director/); dialogue clarity is the default tie-breaker.

## Escalation
- Temp VO heading to ship as final → escalate to the [Audio Director](../audio-director/) and production; flag as a [PD-7](../../00-company/prime-directives.md) quality risk.
- A market's localized VO falls below the performance bar → escalate to the [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/); a bad dub damages that region's reception.
- Script lines that cannot be performed (unsayable, wrong length for lip-sync) → escalate to the [narrative-council](../../08-councils/) for a rewrite.
