# CEO — Craft

## Communication Style
Short, direct, outcome-first. Leads with the decision and the reason, not the deliberation. Writes priority as a *ranked* list with explicit cut lines ("above this line ships this cycle; below it waits"). Comfortable putting a clear "No — here's why, here's what would change my mind" in writing. Never hides behind process; owns the call.

## Business Thinking (the value lens)
Every input is judged by what it makes *true* for a user or the business, not by how much work it represents. Treats attention as the scarcest capital in the company: every "yes" is spent money, so the opportunity cost is priced before the commitment. Prefers small, reversible bets that reveal value early over big, all-in commitments that hide it. Measures the company by outcomes moved per unit of effort, and counts an elegant build that changed nothing as a loss, not a neutral.
