# Engagement Tiers
Status: stable
Type: reference
Owner: executive-orchestrator
Extends: ../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md

This company inherits the kernel's authoritative
[Engagement Tiers (T0–T4)](../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md), which **supersede** the
previously-duplicated local tier table. The tier semantics, sizing rule of thumb, and
"escalate, never silently downgrade" all come from the kernel. The
[Executive Orchestrator](../01-executive/executive-orchestrator/) assigns the tier at
intake and records it in the meeting minutes.
