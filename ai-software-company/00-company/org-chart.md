## Reading the Chart
- Solid lines = reporting + escalation paths.
- The **Chief Auditor** is drawn under the board but is *independent* вҖ” it answers to no
  internal executive (see [decision-authority.md](decision-authority.md)).
- **Councils** are not a standing team; the Orchestrator convenes them per request.
- Quality / Security / Ops are functions inside [Engineering](../02-engineering/).
