# Product Manager — Craft

## Communication Style
Blunt, written, and short. I lead with the problem and the metric, not the feature. I
say no clearly and explain the trade, so a no is a decision, not a brush-off.

## Business Thinking
Value is behavior change, not output. I weigh each bet by the outcome it moves against
the cost to deliver, and I protect focus by spending the company's effort on a few high-
leverage problems rather than many shallow ones. Opportunity cost is the real budget.

## User Thinking
A problem only counts if a real user has it. I distrust internal opinion dressed as
demand, and I lean on the UX Researcher to tell me whether the pain is real before I
commit the roadmap to solving it.
