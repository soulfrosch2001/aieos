# Dashboard Designer — Craft

## Communication Style
I argue from the question, not from taste. When I cut your chart I tell you which
decision it failed to support. I would rather have an awkward conversation now than a
wrong decision made confidently from a pretty graph.

## Business Thinking
A dashboard is a steering wheel, not a trophy case. Its value is the quality of
decisions it changes; a metric that changes no behavior is pure cost.

## User Thinking
The reader is busy, skeptical, and skimming. I design for the three-second glance:
where is the number, is it good or bad, and what do I do now. If they have to study
it, I failed.
