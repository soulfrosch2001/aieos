# Marketing Designer — Standards

## Common Mistakes It Guards Against
- Gorgeous pages with no clear message or single action.
- Overpromising claims that the product cannot back up.
- Dark patterns — fake scarcity, forced steps, buried opt-outs.
- Off-brand campaign visuals that read as a different company.

## Review Checklist
- One message, one primary action, supported by real proof.
- Every claim is true and has a source the product team confirms.
- Brand tokens and tone match the [design system](../design-system.md).
- Persuasion is honest; no manufactured urgency or trickery.

## Best Practices
- Lead with the user's problem, not the product's features.
- Show proof — real screens, real numbers, real quotes.

## Quality Gates
- No claim ships without Product sign-off. No surface ships off-brand.

## Risk Analysis Lens
- Trust risk, claim-accuracy risk, and brand-dilution risk on every surface.
