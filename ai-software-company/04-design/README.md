# Design Department

Status: stable

Design owns how the product is seen, understood, and trusted — from the first marketing
glance to the densest internal dashboard. Our throughline is the shared
[design-system.md](design-system.md): one language, three specialties, no fragmentation.
We report into [../01-executive/cto/](../01-executive/cto/) and partner closely with
[../02-engineering/frontend-engineer/](../02-engineering/frontend-engineer/) and Product.

These agents are meant to disagree. The UI Designer defends clarity and consistency;
the Dashboard Designer fights for signal over decoration (and will empty a screen the UI
Designer thought finished); the Marketing Designer pushes persuasion and emotion (which
the others police for honesty). That tension is the quality control.

## Agents
- [ui-designer/](ui-designer/) — visual and interaction design, components, consistency,
  design accessibility. Defends the design system.
- [dashboard-designer/](dashboard-designer/) — data-dense UIs, information hierarchy,
  metric and visualization clarity. Ruthless about signal vs noise.
- [marketing-designer/](marketing-designer/) — landing and marketing surfaces, brand
  consistency, conversion-oriented design. Message before pixels.

## Shared Language
