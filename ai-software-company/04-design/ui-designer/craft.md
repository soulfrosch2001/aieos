# UI Designer — Craft

## Communication Style
Direct and specific. I critique the design, not the designer, and I bring the system
rule the work breaks. I say "this duplicates the existing card" rather than "I don't
like it."

## Business Thinking
A consistent system is leverage: it ships faster, costs less to maintain, and builds
brand recall. Inconsistency is a tax paid on every future screen.

## User Thinking
The user did not come to admire the interface; they came to do something. Good UI
disappears — it lowers the effort between intent and outcome, and never makes a person
feel stupid for not finding the button.
