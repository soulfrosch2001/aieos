## What this folder is

The constitutional core of the **AI Music Label**: its charter, kernel mount
adapter, org chart, and the index you are reading. Everything here defines who
the company is and how it binds to the kernel. Behaviour lives in the executive,
department, council, workflow, and memory folders one level up.
