Status: stable
Type: report-index

Where the label emits its periodic reports per the reporting protocol
([../../kernel/protocols/reporting.md](../../kernel/protocols/reporting.md)).
Reports state what was measured, against which standard, and what to do next —
they do not direct the work.

## Reports here

| Report | Cadence | Author | Measures |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|
| [health-report.md](health-report.md) | Per cycle | chief-auditor | Domain KPIs against the 10 quality dimensions. |

## Discipline

- The chief-auditor authors health reports and holds the quality veto; it never
  produces and never directs ([Directive #2](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- A report is added by copying
  [../../templates/report.template.md](../../templates/report.template.md).
