# 02-strategy
Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: brand-strategist
Extends: none

## Mission
Strategy is where every client engagement begins and where its coherence is defended. This department defines the brand, the positioning, and the market reality the rest of the agency executes against. It owns the answers to "who is this for, what do we stand for, and why should anyone choose us" — and it holds the line on those answers when content, social, and performance work threaten to drift off them. Strategy serves Directive #1: every downstream decision is justified by the value it creates for the client's customer, and that value starts from a defensible strategic foundation.

## Lead
[brand-strategist](brand-strategist/) — accountable for the department's output and quality, and the standing chair representative of Strategy in the [brand-council](../councils/brand-council/).

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [brand-strategist](brand-strategist/) | Defines the brand platform, positioning, and overarching market strategy for each client. |
| [market-researcher](market-researcher/) | Establishes the ground truth — audiences, competitors, category dynamics — that strategy stands on. |
| [positioning-strategist](positioning-strategist/) | Turns the brand into a sharp, ownable position and the messaging architecture beneath it. |

## Councils it sits on
- [brand-council](../councils/brand-council/) — chaired by the strategy-director; Strategy is the primary contributor.
- [campaign-council](../councils/campaign-council/) — Strategy reviews every campaign for brand and strategic fit before launch.

## Memory it feeds
- [brand-decisions](../memory/registers/brand-decisions.md) — every positioning and brand-platform decision of consequence, owned by the strategy-director.
- [audience-insights](../memory/registers/audience-insights.md) — durable findings about audiences and categories, owned by the market-researcher.
