# AI Marketing Agency — Company Core
Status: stable
Type: company
Owner: ceo
Extends: kernel + stdlib

## What this folder is
The governance core of the AI Marketing Agency — a full-service marketing agency
delivering brand strategy, content, social, and performance marketing for clients.
This folder holds the company's charter, its kernel mount adapter, and its org
chart. The company is **kernel-native**: born inheriting AIEOS, forking nothing
(Directive [#6](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Index
- [COMPANY.md](COMPANY.md) — the charter: what the company inherits from the

  and three departments.
