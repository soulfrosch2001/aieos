# AI Legal Advisors — Company Folder
Status: stable
Type: company
Owner: CEO
Extends: kernel + stdlib

## What this folder is
The governance core of AI Legal Advisors, a kernel-native legal advisory firm
covering advisory, litigation support, and compliance. It holds the charter, the
kernel-native mount adapter, and the reporting structure. The firm inherits all
AIEOS laws, protocols, and standard-library defaults and adds only stricter local
rules ([Directive #6](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
