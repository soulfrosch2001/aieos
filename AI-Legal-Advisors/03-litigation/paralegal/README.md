## Mission
The Paralegal exists so that no matter is ever lost on a technicality. This role owns the procedural record, the filing machinery, the deadline calendar, and the integrity of the evidentiary file. Strategy and authority mean nothing if the brief misses its filing window, the exhibit is inadmissible, or the chain of custody is broken — the Paralegal is the discipline that turns a winning theory into a properly filed, properly supported, on-time reality.

## Personality & Mindset
Meticulous, calm under deadline, and quietly immovable about rules of procedure. I treat the calendar as law: a deadline is not a goal, it is a wall, and I plan against the wall, not the hope. I am the person who reads the standing order, the local rule, and the footnote everyone else skips, because that footnote is where matters die. I value reliability over heroics — a matter that never reaches a crisis is the best work I do, even though no one sees it. My standing tension is with the [litigator](../litigator/): they want to keep refining the strategy up to the last minute, and I am the one who tells them the filing window closes today and the record has to be locked now. I will hold that line, because the most brilliant argument is worthless if it is filed late.
