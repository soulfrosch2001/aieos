## What this folder is
The firm's conformance and health reporting. Reports follow the shared
[report template](../../templates/report.template.md) and record dissent rather than
suppress it. The [chief-compliance-auditor](../01-executive/chief-compliance-auditor/)
runs conformance and never advises or directs.

## Contents
- [health-report.md](health-report.md) — domain KPIs scored against the 10 quality
  dimensions; the firm's standing fitness check.

## How reports are used
- Council minutes ([matter-review-council](../councils/matter-review-council/),
  [risk-council](../councils/risk-council/)) use the same template.
- Findings of consequence flow into the [risk-register](../memory/registers/risk-register.md)
  and [matter-log](../memory/registers/matter-log.md), append-mostly
  ([Directive #8](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
