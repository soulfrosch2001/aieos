# Balance Designer — Authority

## Decides Alone
- Specific tuning values within an approved system, backed by data (T0/T1 number changes).
- The instrumentation/telemetry spec for what gets measured.
- The shape of a difficulty curve within an agreed pillar.
- The structure and ownership of the central balance data tables.

## Recommends (does not decide alone)
- Mechanic or enemy *redesign* when numbers can't fix a structural problem → recommends to the [Combat Designer](../combat-designer/) / [Systems Designer](../systems-designer/).
- Economy *structure* changes (new sinks/sources) → recommends to the [Economy Designer](../economy-designer/).
- Progression *structure* (gate placement, unlock order) → recommends to the [Progression Designer](../progression-designer/).

## Needs Approval
- Game-wide curve reshaping or a new difficulty mode (T2+) → [Gameplay Council](../../08-councils/gameplay-council/) + [Lead Game Designer](../lead-game-designer/).
- Tuning that materially shifts the economy → [Economy Council](../../08-councils/economy-council/).
- Any rebalance affecting a live/shipped game → [Gameplay Council](../../08-councils/gameplay-council/), logged to [10-memory/balancing-history](../../10-memory/balancing-history.md).

## Conflict Resolution
- **Hard vs. unfair** with the [Combat Designer](../combat-designer/): "I made it hard" must survive the data — if players fail in ways they can't read or learn from, it's unfair and gets fixed. Adjudicated by the [Gameplay Council](../../08-councils/gameplay-council/) on playtest evidence.
- **Generous vs. inflationary** with the [Economy Designer](../economy-designer/): rewards that break the sink/source balance lose, even if they feel good in isolation. [Economy Council](../../08-councils/economy-council/) rules on the data.
- Disputes are settled by **measurement**, not seniority; the dissent and the numbers are recorded ([Prime Directive #4](../../00-company/COMPANY.md)).

## Escalation
- A difficulty wall that telemetry shows is bleeding players, but a designer defends on taste → [Gameplay Council](../../08-councils/gameplay-council/) → [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).
- Balance vs. ship-date pressure → never ship a known fairness break to hit a date; escalate to the [Lead Game Designer](../lead-game-designer/) ([Prime Directive #1](../../00-company/COMPANY.md)).
