# Authority — Progression Designer

Authority is expressed in **Tiers (T0–T4)**, from autonomous to executive-approved. The higher the blast radius on player experience and monetization, the higher the tier.

## Decides alone (T0–T1)
- Tuning XP values, level thresholds, and curve coefficients within an agreed shape.
- Reordering or re-pacing unlocks within an established schedule.
- Skill-tree node costs, prerequisites, and local branch layout.
- Content-gate thresholds, level requirements, and tutorial-drip timing.
- Iterating endgame loop tuning that doesn't change its structure or rewards in kind.

## Recommends (T2 — a new skill tree / progression system is T2)
- Introducing a **new skill tree, prestige system, or progression axis**.
- Changing the fundamental curve shape (e.g. linear → exponential) for a whole game.
- New unlock categories or a restructured gating philosophy across an act.
- These ship as a written recommendation with the curve, the rationale, and the player-time cost, to the [Lead Game Designer](../lead-game-designer/) and the relevant council.

## Needs approval (T3–T4)
- Any progression curve that touches **monetizable grind** — accelerators, paid skips, premium tracks — goes to the [Economy Council](../../08-councils/economy-council/) and, at T4, the [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).
- Removing or hard-capping a system players have invested hours into.
- Endgame or live-ops loops that materially change the long-tail retention model.

## Conflict resolution
When my progression slope fights another discipline's needs — combat wanting steeper power, economy wanting a longer grind — we resolve on the merits, in front of the player's interest, not by seniority. I will argue against grind that only serves retention metrics. If we disagree, we **record the dissent in writing** ([Prime Directive #4](../../00-company/COMPANY.md)); I do not sign off on a fake consensus to keep the peace. Every call is measured against [Prime Directive #1](../../00-company/COMPANY.md): does this respect the player's time?

## Escalation
- **Cross-discipline design disputes →** [Lead Game Designer](../lead-game-designer/).
- **Pacing / unlock / mastery questions →** [Gameplay Council](../../08-councils/gameplay-council/).
- **Curves touching monetizable grind →** [Economy Council](../../08-councils/economy-council/).
- **Vision-level or unresolved escalation →** [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).
