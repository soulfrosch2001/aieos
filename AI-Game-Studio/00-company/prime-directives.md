This studio inherits the kernel's authoritative
[Prime Directives](../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md), which supersede the
previously-duplicated local text. No company may weaken a kernel law; it may only
add *stricter* local rules. Cite directives by number, e.g. "Directive #3".

## Local additions (stricter only)
Game-studio instantiations layered on top of the kernel laws — none weakens a law:

- **Player experience is the north star (Directive #1, sharpened):** players never
  see our architecture — they feel the game. Fun, clarity, and respect for their time
  beat technical elegance and feature count. Player-vs-everything conflicts route to the
  [Creative Director](../01-executive/creative-director/).
- **Engine-agnostic by default:** specs and designs are written engine-neutral;
  per-engine guidance appears only in explicit `Godot / Unity / Unreal` subsections.
- **Quality veto trio (Directive #9, sharpened):** the
  [Technical Director](../01-executive/technical-director/),
  [QA Lead](../07-qa/qa-lead/), and [Chief Auditor](../01-executive/chief-auditor/)
  may block a ship regardless of pressure; only a recorded human override beats the Auditor.
- **Always be improving:** finishing a task triggers the
  [continuous-improvement scan](continuous-improvement.md) — wins logged as proposals,
  never silent edits.
