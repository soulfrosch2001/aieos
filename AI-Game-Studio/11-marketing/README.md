# 📣 11-marketing — Marketing & Community Department

`Status: stable`

> The studio's voice to the outside world — and the player's voice back into the studio.
> Anchored in Prime Directive #1: player experience is the north star
> ([../00-company/prime-directives.md](../00-company/prime-directives.md)).

## Mission
Marketing exists so the right players find a game they'll genuinely love, arrive with
accurate expectations, and stay as a healthy community. We are not a hype machine. We
refuse to oversell features the studio hasn't committed to build (Prime Directive #3:
decide before you build) and we treat player trust as the compounding asset it is. When
this department does its job, wishlists convert because the promise matched the game; when
it cuts corners, refunds spike, reviews sour, and the community goes quiet. Every outward
asset must honestly represent the real player experience — a bullshot or a misleading tag
is a Prime Directive #1 violation, not a clever tactic.

## How The Department Connects
- **Player feedback loop →** the [community-manager](community-manager/) triages sentiment
  into [../10-memory/community-feedback.md](../10-memory/community-feedback.md), then carries
  it into the [../08-councils/release-council/](../08-councils/release-council/) Go/No-Go.
- **Beats follow the plan →** campaign timing is pinned to milestones in
  [../10-memory/roadmap.md](../10-memory/roadmap.md); we never announce what isn't real.
- **The studio remembers →** every significant positioning, launch-window, or experiment
  decision updates [../10-memory/](../10-memory/) (Prime Directive #5). Concluded A/B tests
  write a lesson to [../10-memory/lessons-learned.md](../10-memory/lessons-learned.md).
- **Quality has veto →** QA and the release council can block the "launch" toggle on the
  store page regardless of marketing pressure (Prime Directive #7).

## Operating Principle
Disagreement is a feature here too (Prime Directive #4): the
[store-optimization-specialist](store-optimization-specialist/) will challenge the
[marketing-director](marketing-director/)'s favorite capsule with real conversion data, and
that tension is exactly the point. Honest signal beats flattering noise — every time.
