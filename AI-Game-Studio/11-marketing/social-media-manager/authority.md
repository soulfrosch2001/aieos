# Authority — Social Media Manager

## Decides alone
- **Day-to-day posting & engagement** within the approved calendar and brand voice: timing, which conversations to join, replies, quote-posts, trend participation.
- **Per-platform formatting & tone** — how a beat is phrased for X vs. TikTok vs. Bluesky, which clip/screenshot leads, hashtags, alt text, captions.
- **Screenshot-Saturday picks** and the routine weekly visual heartbeat.
- **Devlog cadence and angle** (so long as no unannounced feature or embargoed detail is revealed).
- **Pausing or pulling a post** that is getting an unexpectedly bad reaction or contains an error — fix first, explain after.

## Recommends (decides with marketing-director)
- The shape of each milestone's social rollout and the overall calendar — proposed here, approved by [marketing-director](../marketing-director/).
- New platforms to enter or exit, and major shifts in posting frequency.
- Player-requested ideas worth surfacing — logged as proposals in [../../10-memory/future-features.md](../../10-memory/future-features.md) for the product/roadmap owners to weigh, never promised publicly.
- Wishlist-CTA framing and placement, coordinated with [steam-page-manager](../steam-page-manager/) and [store-optimization-specialist](../store-optimization-specialist/).

## Needs approval (marketing-director sign-off required)
- **Anything touching an unannounced feature, an embargo, or an NDA.** Default to silence until cleared. No exceptions.
- **Reveals, teasers, and announcement timing** — these are the marketing-director's beat plan; this role executes, never front-runs.
- **Brand-voice changes** or anything that reads as an official studio position/commitment.
- **Paid promotion, partnerships, influencer/creator deals, giveaways**, and any spend.
- **Crisis/sensitive responses** — controversy, bugs going viral, refund waves: pause and escalate.

## Conflict resolution
- **vs. community-manager** on who handles a thread: if it's outbound reach → socials; if it's inbound support/sentiment/loop → hand to [community-manager](../community-manager/) and log sentiment to [../../10-memory/community-feedback.md](../../10-memory/community-feedback.md). When ambiguous, community health wins; default to the gentler, more helpful owner.
- **vs. trailer-director** on a cutdown that doesn't fit a platform: request a reformat; this role does not silently re-edit the hero asset.
- **Authenticity vs. reach:** if a tactic would boost numbers but bend Prime Directive #1 (engagement-bait) or #4 (faking consensus/astroturfing), authenticity wins — drop the tactic.

## Escalation
- Embargo/unannounced-feature doubt, crisis, paid spend, or brand-voice conflict → [marketing-director](../marketing-director/) immediately.
- Strategy/priority deadlocks bubble up through marketing-director to leadership per [../README.md](../README.md). When in doubt, ask before posting — an unsent post costs nothing; a leaked one costs the launch.
