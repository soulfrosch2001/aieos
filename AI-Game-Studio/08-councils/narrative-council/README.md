# 📖 Narrative Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: chair
Extends: none

`Status: stable`

> Guards the fiction and its coherence: **does the story earn the player's belief, and does
> it hold together as they pull on it?** This is **Prime Directive #1** — player experience
> ([../../00-company/prime-directives.md](../../00-company/prime-directives.md)) — applied to
> the place where authored story meets player agency.

## Purpose
Decide the story, the world and its lore, dialogue quality, and character arcs — and the
hardest question of all: **how narrative meshes with player agency**, where authored beats
end and systemic emergence begins. The council owns *the fiction and whether it stays
consistent* across quests, characters, and tonal shifts. It does **not** own how mechanics
*feel* — that defers to the [gameplay-council](../gameplay-council/) — but it does insist
that what the player does and what the story claims never contradict each other.

## Participants
- **Chair:** [narrative-designer](../../02-design/narrative-designer/) — final say on story, lore, and tone.
- **Core members:**
  - [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) — vision keeper and the tie-break on fiction.
  - [quest-designer](../../02-design/quest-designer/) — how story becomes playable structure and branches.
  - [voice-director](../../05-audio/voice-director/) — dialogue quality, performance, and character voice.
- **Advisory (as needed):** [lead-game-designer](../../02-design/lead-game-designer/) for
  story-vs-systemic tension, [level-designer](../../02-design/level-designer/) for
  environmental storytelling, and [community-manager](../../11-marketing/community-manager/)
  for lore and community impact.

## When Convened
- **T2** — a new quest line, character, or narrative system (e.g. a dialogue or reputation framework).
- **T3** — a story pillar, major arc, or tonal shift → council **+** executive board;
  the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) signs off on vision.
- Not convened for **T0/T1** line edits or barks (the specialist or
  [narrative-designer](../../02-design/narrative-designer/) handles it).
