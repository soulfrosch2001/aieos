# 🎨 Art Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: chair
Extends: none

`Status: stable`

> Guards the question every frame answers: **does this look like our game, and does it
> meet the bar?** Visual identity is **Prime Directive #1** ([../../00-company/prime-directives.md](../../00-company/prime-directives.md))
> made into a decision body — the player feels the art before they read a word.

## Purpose
Decide the visual direction, style consistency, and the art quality bar — and whether a
given asset or look genuinely serves the game's identity. The council owns *how the game
looks and holds together visually*; it does **not** own technical render cost — that lives
with the [performance-council](../performance-council/) and [technical-council](../technical-council/).
But visual ambition is never blank-cheque: every verdict here respects the frame and memory
budget, and the [technical-artist](../../04-art/technical-artist/) is the standing bridge
between what the art wants and what the engine can afford.

## Participants
- **Chair:** [art-director](../../04-art/art-director/) — final say on visual direction.
- **Core members:**
  - [concept-artist](../../04-art/concept-artist/) — the look before it's built; explores the space.
  - [character-artist](../../04-art/character-artist/) — silhouette, readability, and character fidelity.
  - [environment-artist](../../04-art/environment-artist/) — world cohesion, scale, and mood.
  - [technical-artist](../../04-art/technical-artist/) — bridges art ambition to the frame/memory budget.
  - [ui-artist](../../04-art/ui-artist/) — interface clarity and visual language consistency.
- **Advisory (as needed):** [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) for
  identity conflicts, [lighting-artist](../../04-art/lighting-artist/) for mood and tone,
  [vfx-artist](../../04-art/vfx-artist/) for motion and effects readability.

## When Convened
- **T2** — a new asset class, or a style change inside one department.
- **T3** — a new art-direction pillar → council **+** executive board,
  [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/) signs off.
- Not convened for **T0/T1** polish (the specialist or [art-director](../../04-art/art-director/) handles it).
