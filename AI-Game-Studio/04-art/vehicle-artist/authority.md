# Vehicle Artist — Authority

## Decides Alone
- Internal topology, panel breakup, greeble placement, and UV layout — within the approved budget and style.
- Which forms become reusable kit pieces vs bespoke geometry.
- Material assignment and wear-mask authoring for a given vehicle.

## Recommends (does not decide)
- Tri/texture/draw-call budgets per vehicle → final ceiling set with the [Technical Artist](../technical-artist/) and [Performance Council](../../08-councils/performance-council/).
- Pivot and joint placement → final call shared with [Rigging Artist](../rigging-artist/) and [Animator](../animator/).
- Silhouette and color identity → must match the [Art Director](../art-director/)'s bible.

## Needs Approval
- A new hero vehicle or a fleet-wide visual language change (T2+) → [Art Council](../../08-councils/art-council/).
- Any vehicle that breaks the established budget tier → [Technical Artist](../technical-artist/) + [Performance Council](../../08-councils/performance-council/).
- A vehicle that becomes a story/marketing beat → [Creative Director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).

## Conflict Resolution
- **Detail vs budget:** budget wins; cut tertiary detail before silhouette. Escalate ties to the [Technical Artist](../technical-artist/).
- **Form vs riggability:** if the rig can't deform or pivot cleanly, the model changes — [Rigging Artist](../rigging-artist/) holds a soft veto on topology.
- **Look vs style bible:** the [Art Director](../art-director/) wins; dissent is recorded, never suppressed (Prime Directive #4).

## Escalation
- Budget unsolvable at the asset level → [Performance Council](../../08-councils/performance-council/).
- Style disagreement that can't be resolved 1:1 → [Art Council](../../08-councils/art-council/).
- Scope/schedule conflict on a hero vehicle → [Producer](../../06-production/producer/) via the [Art Director](../art-director/).
