# Creative Council
Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: creative-director
Extends: architecture-council

## Purpose
Adjudicates **creative and story direction** when it cannot be settled inside one
department — tone, theme, character truth, the shape of a sequence, and the
coherence of vision across films. It answers: *is this the right creative direction,
and what are we deliberately trading away to get it?* It does **not** greenlight
(that is the [greenlight-council](../greenlight-council/README.md)), set schedule,
or cut the film — a council decides, it never builds (Directive
[#2](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)). It inherits the stdlib
[architecture-council](../../../councils/architecture-council/README.md) and
overrides it for creative coherence (Directive
[#5](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks):
  [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/README.md) — owns story
  and creative coherence; holds the veto on off-vision work.
- **Core** (must attend): the leads in dispute — typically
  [director](../../03-production/director/README.md),
  [screenwriter](../../02-development/screenwriter/README.md), and
  [editor](../../04-post/editor/README.md).
- **Advisory** (as needed):
  [story-editor](../../02-development/story-editor/README.md),
  [vfx-supervisor](../../04-post/vfx-supervisor/README.md),
  [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/README.md) (process veto, never
  directs).

## When convened
At [T3](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) — a cross-department creative bet
or an unresolved direction dispute. Convened for one question, then disbanded; not
standing. T3 requires council **plus** executive sign-off, per
[engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md).

## Inputs
- The creative question and the two (or more) directions in dispute, each with its
  rationale — a single option is not a debate.
- The material under dispute (script pages, the cut in question, the design).
- The greenlit vision the film must honour, from
  [greenlight-decisions](../../memory/registers/greenlight-decisions.md).
