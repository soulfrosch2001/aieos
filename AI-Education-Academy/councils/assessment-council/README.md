# Assessment Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: assessment-designer
Extends: feature-council

## Purpose

The assessment council approves assessments and verifies their alignment to learning outcomes: what is assessed, how, the standard for passing, and whether each instrument actually measures the outcome it claims. It never sets the outcomes themselves (that is curriculum's domain), never authors the teaching content, and never schedules cohorts. It owns the question "does this assessment prove the learner met the outcome?"

## Participants

- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [assessment-designer](../../02-curriculum/assessment-designer/).
- **Core** (must attend): [curriculum-designer](../../02-curriculum/curriculum-designer/), [instructional-designer](../../02-curriculum/instructional-designer/), [instructor](../../04-delivery/instructor/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [learning-experience-designer](../../04-delivery/learning-experience-designer/), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).

## When convened

Convened at **T2+** per [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md): new or substantially revised assessments, changes to passing standards, or outcome-alignment disputes. T0–T1 tweaks stay with the assessment-designer. Not standing.

## Inputs

- The assessment and the specific outcome(s) it claims to measure.
- Evidence of alignment and any known validity concerns.
- Relevant entries from [learning-outcomes](../../memory/registers/learning-outcomes.md) and live delivery signals from the [instructor](../../04-delivery/instructor/).
