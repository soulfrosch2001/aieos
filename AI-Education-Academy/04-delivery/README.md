# Delivery

Status: stable
Type: department
Owner: instructor
Extends: none

## Mission

Delivery is where the academy meets the learner. We take finished curriculum and content and turn it into a lived learning experience: cohorts that run on time, sessions that land, feedback loops that catch confusion before it compounds. We are the last mile and the first signal — the place where every upstream decision is tested against a real human trying to learn.

## Charter

- Run cohorts and sessions on schedule, at quality, with humane pacing.
- Observe what actually happens to learners and feed it back to curriculum and content.
- Improve the experience continuously — friction removed, comprehension raised, completion sustained.

## Members

- [instructor](instructor/) — department lead; delivers sessions, reads the room, owns live learner outcomes.
- [learning-experience-designer](learning-experience-designer/) — designs the end-to-end learner journey, environment, and feedback instrumentation.

## Reports to

- [operations-director](../01-executive/operations-director/) — owns delivery sequencing, cohorts, and schedule (COO authority).
- Pedagogical coherence is owned by [academic-director](../01-executive/academic-director/), who holds a teaching/quality veto.
