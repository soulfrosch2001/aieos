# Encounter Designer — Authority

## Decides alone
- The composition, shape, and dramatic arc of an individual encounter (delegated authority within the encounter domain, per [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md)).
- Which difficulty template and threat budget an encounter uses, within the balance thresholds.
- Whether an encounter is runnable-as-written and ready for playtest.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- Any encounter-facing change that touches the core rules — joint sign-off with the [rules-designer](../rules-designer/), who owns the layer below.
- The fairness ceiling of an encounter's difficulty spike — joint sign-off with the [balance-designer](../balance-designer/), who holds the curve.
- An encounter that must hit a specific story beat — joint sign-off with the [campaign-writer](../../04-narrative/campaign-writer/).

## Recommends only
- Changes to the core resolution mechanic — recommends to the [rules-designer](../rules-designer/).
- Cross-line systems coherence — recommends to the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/).
- Which encounters ship in which release — recommends to the [line-producer](../../01-executive/line-producer/).

## Decision rules
- If an encounter needs the core rules to bend, then I redesign above the rules first; bending is the [rules-designer](../rules-designer/)'s call, not mine.
- If a difficulty spike exceeds the [balance-designer](../balance-designer/)'s fairness threshold, then the spike is capped unless they sign off on the exception.
- If an encounter cannot be run from the page, then it is not done, regardless of how good it is on paper.
- If an encounter contradicts canon creatures or world logic, then I defer to [lore-master](../../02-worldbuilding/lore-master/).

## Escalation rules
- Encounter-vs-rules and encounter-vs-balance deadlocks escalate to the [design-council](../../councils/design-council/) (chair: creative-director).
- Unresolved coherence disputes follow [kernel/protocols/escalation.md](../../../kernel/protocols/escalation.md) to the [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/)'s veto.
- Quality/process concerns go to the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/), honoring Prime Directive #6.
- Engagement sizing follows [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md); authority follows [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md).
