# Lore Council

Status: stable
Type: council
Owner: lore-master
Extends: feature-council

## Purpose
The Lore Council approves additions to the world's canon — new factions, history,
geography, cosmology, and the facts campaigns and quests are allowed to assert as true. It
guards a single, coherent world. It does not decide how systems play (that is the
[design-council](../design-council/)'s room), does not write campaigns, and does not own
release scope.

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks): [lore-master](../../02-worldbuilding/lore-master/).
- **Core** (must attend): [cartographer](../../02-worldbuilding/cartographer/), [faction-designer](../../02-worldbuilding/faction-designer/), [campaign-writer](../../04-narrative/campaign-writer/).
- **Advisory** (as needed): [quest-designer](../../04-narrative/quest-designer/), [rules-designer](../../03-systems/rules-designer/), [creative-director](../../01-executive/creative-director/), [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/).

## When convened
Convened for T2+ canon additions or changes — see
[engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). Not standing; the
[rpg-orchestrator](../../01-executive/rpg-orchestrator/) convenes it when a proposal would
add or alter world canon.

## Inputs
- The proposed canon addition and its motivation (often a campaign or quest need).
- The current [canon](../../memory/registers/canon.md) register, to check for contradiction.
- Any map, faction, or timeline artifacts the addition touches.
