## What is here
This folder holds the studio's foundational documents: its charter, its
kernel-native mount adapter, its organization chart, and the local rules that
sit (strictly) on top of the inherited kernel.

AI RPG Studio is a tabletop RPG studio — worldbuilding, game systems, and
narrative campaigns — born inheriting the [AIEOS kernel](../../kernel/). It forks
nothing.

## Index
- [COMPANY.md](COMPANY.md) — charter: what the studio does, what it inherits, and
  the stricter local rules it adds.
- [AIEOS.md](AIEOS.md) — kernel-native mount adapter: executive-to-authority
  mapping, local overrides, and register mapping (`Requires kernel: ^1.0.0`).
- [org-chart.md](org-chart.md) — reporting structure across the five executives
  and three departments.
