# Design Council
Status: draft
Type: council
Owner: design-director
Extends: feature-council ([../../../councils/feature-council/README.md](../../../councils/feature-council/README.md))

## Purpose
The room where a **core design decision is argued before a single component is
prototyped** — the studio's binding of "discuss before you build" (Directive
[#3](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)). It decides *what game we are
making and why this shape over the alternatives*: the core loop, the win
condition, the central tension a player feels. It does **not** write rules text,
tune numbers (that is [balance-council](../balance-council/README.md)), or
relitigate the studio's mandate set above it.

## Charter
One design proposal at [T2](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) or above — a
new game, mode, or system with real design choices. One question, one council.
A T3 design (a new product line) inherits this council and adds executive
sign-off; it does not fork a new one (Directive
[#6](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Participants
- **Chair** (breaks deadlocks, holds the design veto): design-director — owns
  design coherence across the studio.
- **Core** (must attend): lead-game-designer, systems-designer, the
  narrative-designer when the decision touches theme or fiction.
- **Advisory** (as needed): balance-designer when a structural choice carries a
  fairness cost, producer for scope, chief-auditor for the quality gate.

## When convened
At [T2 and above](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md) — see
[engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md). Convened, not
standing (Directive [#3](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).

## Inputs
- The player experience the design must create — no target feeling, no council.
- At least two candidate designs to compare (one option is not a debate).
- The constraints: component budget, table footprint, target play time, age band.
