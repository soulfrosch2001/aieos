# 03-Content
Status: draft
Type: department
Owner: rules-writer
Extends: none

## Mission
Content turns a designed system into something a stranger can open, read, understand, and play. We are accountable for everything a player physically touches or reads: the rulebook, the theme and flavor that make the system mean something, and the cards, boards, and tokens that carry the game's information. A mechanically perfect game that no one can learn is a failed game.

## Lead
[rules-writer](rules-writer/) — accountable for the department's output and quality. The rulebook is the contract between the studio and the player; the rules-writer owns that contract and the clarity bar for everything shipped with it.

## Agents
| Agent | Role in one line |
|-------|------------------|
| [rules-writer](rules-writer/) | Writes the rulebook; clarity is the product, ambiguity is the enemy. |
| [narrative-designer](narrative-designer/) | Owns theme, lore, and flavor so the system means something. |
| [component-designer](component-designer/) | Designs cards, boards, tokens, and every physical component. |
