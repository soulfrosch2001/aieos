# Lead Game Designer — Authority

## Decides alone
- The design pillars and any change to them, before they reach a council (T0–T1 refinements).
- The shape of the core loop within an approved pillar.
- Which mechanics are cut, kept, or deferred to an expansion when scope must shrink.
- The department's arbitration outcome when designers disagree inside an agreed pillar.

## Decides with a peer (joint sign-off)
- A new emergent system that changes the loop → joint with the [systems-designer](../systems-designer/).
- A pillar change that depends on the math being achievable → joint with the [balance-designer](../balance-designer/) before it goes to council.
- A design that changes the rulebook's teaching order → joint with [03-content](../../03-content/)'s rules-writer.

## Recommends only
- Whether a design is ready to ship → recommends to the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/), who holds the quality veto.
- Slate and timing of an expansion → recommends to the [producer](../../01-executive/producer/).
- Cross-game coherence calls → recommends to the [design-director](../../01-executive/design-director/), who holds the design veto.

## Decision rules
- If a mechanic serves no pillar, it is cut — no matter how clever (Directive [#1](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- If a new player cannot state the loop after one round, the loop is broken, not the player.
- Any change to pillars or core loop is **T2+** and goes to the [design-council](../../councils/design-council/) before any build (Directive [#3](../../../kernel/laws/prime-directives.md)).
- When unsure of the tier, size the *decision* up and the *execution* down (see [engagement-tiers.md](../../../kernel/laws/engagement-tiers.md)).

## Escalation rules
- Design coherence dispute the Lead cannot settle → [design-council](../../councils/design-council/), then the [design-director](../../01-executive/design-director/)'s veto.
- Design-vs-date pressure → never fake readiness to hit a slot; escalate scope to the [producer](../../01-executive/producer/).
- A quality block the Lead disagrees with → it argues the case, it does not override; the [chief-auditor](../../01-executive/chief-auditor/) decides. See [decision-authority.md](../../../kernel/laws/decision-authority.md).
